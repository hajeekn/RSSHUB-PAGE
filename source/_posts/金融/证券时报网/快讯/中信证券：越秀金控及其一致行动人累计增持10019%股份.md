<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600030" target="_blank">中信证券</a>(600030)2月17日晚间公告，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000987" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000987" target="_blank">越秀金控</a>及全资子公司暨一致行动人通过二级市场集中竞价、参与A股配股增持<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600030" target="_blank">中信证券</a>股份，且因<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600030" target="_blank">中信证券</a>H股配股尚未完成导致所持股比被动上升，累计增持达1.0019%。此次权益变动后，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000987" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="000987" target="_blank">越秀金控</a>及其一致行动人持有公司股份比例从6.2650%增至7.2669%。</p>
