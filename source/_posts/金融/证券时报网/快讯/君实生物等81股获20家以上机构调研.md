<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（2月23日至3月1日）沪深两市约205家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有81家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到650家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688180" code="688180">君实生物</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688005" code="688005">容百科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688012" code="688012">中微公司</a>等分别被466家、266家、251家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，有3家公司被机构调研3次，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300007" code="300007">汉威科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300083" code="300083">创世纪</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002488" code="002488">金固股份</a>等，机构调研最为密集。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有17只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300316" code="300316">晶盛机电</a>近5日净流入资金1.24亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000657" code="000657">中钨高新</a>等。</p>
