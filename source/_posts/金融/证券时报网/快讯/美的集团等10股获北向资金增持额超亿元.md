<p>证券时报网讯，据wind统计显示，3月21日共有529只个股获北向资金持股量环比上一个交易日增加。以增持的股份数量和当日收盘价为基准进行测算，加仓股中，增持市值在1亿元以上的有10只，增持市值最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000333" code="000333">美的集团</a>，最新持股量为11.69亿股，环比增加0.3%，增持市值为2.07亿元；增持市值较多还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002008" code="002008">大族激光</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002460" code="002460">赣锋锂业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600588" code="600588">用友网络</a>等股。</p><p>从机构动向看，北向资金增持市值居前个股，近一个月获机构调研的有8只，机构调研次数较为频繁的有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688005" code="688005">容百科技</a>等，期间分别获机构调研2次；机构调研家数看，机构较为扎堆的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" code="300124">汇川技术</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688005" code="688005">容百科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601012" code="601012">隆基股份</a>。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202203/W020220322345478599127.png" title="北向资金增持市值排名.png" alt="北向资金增持市值排名.png" oldsrc="W020220322345478599127.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
