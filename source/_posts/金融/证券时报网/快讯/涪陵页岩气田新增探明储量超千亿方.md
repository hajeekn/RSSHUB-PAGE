<p>证券时报e公司讯，据<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600028" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600028" target="_blank">中国石化</a>消息，我国首个大型页岩气田<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600028" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600028" target="_blank">中国石化</a>涪陵页岩气田白马区块页岩气新增探明储量1048.83亿立方米，至此，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600028" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600028" target="_blank">中国石化</a>江汉油田涪陵页岩气田累计探明储量近9000亿立方米，占全国页岩气探明储量的34%。</p>
