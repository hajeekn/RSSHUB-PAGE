<p>证券时报e公司讯，12月21日，中国商业技师协会“首届CABP服务业数字化转型推进会”暨<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币联合推广签约仪式在北京采用<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300959" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300959" target="_blank">线上线下</a>融合形式召开。<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币联合推广签约仪式上，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603825" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603825" target="_blank">华扬联众</a>副总裁贾建萍代表<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603825" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603825" target="_blank">华扬联众</a>与<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601398" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601398" target="_blank">工商银行</a>签署“<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币封闭实验框架协议”，未来双方将整合用户、市场、渠道、数据和技术等资源，联合开展<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币封闭试验合作，共同加速场景建设和用户推广，树立金融同业深度合作典范。</p>
