<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（3月18日至3月24日）沪深两市约125家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有50家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" code="300124">汇川技术</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到392家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002865" code="002865">钧达股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000729" code="000729">燕京啤酒</a>等分别被346家、161家、132家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，有2家公司被机构调研5次，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002549" code="002549">凯美特气</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000729" code="000729">燕京啤酒</a>。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有15只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300474" code="300474">景嘉微</a>近5日净流入资金0.67亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" code="300124">汇川技术</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002915" code="002915">中欣氟材</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202203/W020220325342849637232.png" title="近5日机构调研股一览.png" alt="近5日机构调研股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220325342849637232.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
