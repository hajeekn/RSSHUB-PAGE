<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（2月28日至3月4日）沪深两市约173家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有69家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002906" code="002906">华阳集团</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到328家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688008" code="688008">澜起科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688012" code="688012">中微公司</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688556" code="688556">高测股份</a>等分别被309家、251家、246家机构调研。机构调研次数来看，有2家公司被机构调研3次，分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000552" code="000552">靖远煤电</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300636" code="300636">同和药业</a>，机构调研最为密集。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有23只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301235" code="301235">华康医疗</a>近5日净流入资金1.29亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300498" code="300498">温氏股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688621" code="688621">阳光诺和</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202203/W020220307334306723391.png" title="近5日机构调研股一览.png" alt="近5日机构调研股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220307334306723391.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
