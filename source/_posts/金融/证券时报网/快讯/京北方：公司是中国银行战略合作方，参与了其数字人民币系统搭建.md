<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002987" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="002987" target="_blank">京北方</a>在机构调研时表示，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601988" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601988" target="_blank">中国银行</a>是奥运会唯一银行合作伙伴，公司是<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601988" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="601988" target="_blank">中国银行</a>战略合作方，参与了其<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币系统搭建。目前公司业务已经介入数字货币场景160多类，渗透<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币各层生态圈，覆盖各类场景，服务支撑能力强。公司在<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币领域技术储备逐渐成熟的同时，业务也涉及<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币发行流通、支付结算全流程，形成了完整的解决方案，有<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币统一接入平台、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币运营管理系统、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币钱包应用系统等，支持同业金融机构快速与九大<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="835670" target="_blank">数字人</a>民币运营商对接，未来这将为公司带来更多业务。</p>
