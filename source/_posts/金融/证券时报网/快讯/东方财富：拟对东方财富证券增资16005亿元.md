<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300059" target="_blank">东方财富</a>(300059)3月18日晚间公告，公司现以对<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300059" target="_blank">东方财富</a>证券的债权157.73亿元及现金2.32亿元，对<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300059" target="_blank">东方财富</a>证券进行增资，增资总额160.05亿元。上述债权为公司以可转债募集资金向<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300059" target="_blank">东方财富</a>证券提供的借款及利息。上述增资总额中，33.00亿元作为<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300059" target="_blank">东方财富</a>证券注册资本，127.05亿元作为资本公积。增资完成后，<a class="trsHotWords" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300059" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300059" target="_blank">东方财富</a>证券注册资本由88.00亿元增至121.00亿元。</p>
