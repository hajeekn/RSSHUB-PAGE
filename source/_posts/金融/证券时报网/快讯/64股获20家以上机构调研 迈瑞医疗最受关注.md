<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近5个交易日（12月30日至1月6日）沪深两市约189家上市公司被机构调研。机构调研榜单中，共有64家公司获20家以上机构扎堆调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300760" code="300760">迈瑞医疗</a>最受关注，参与调研的机构达到649家；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688518" code="688518">联赢激光</a>被455家机构调研，榜单中排名第二。机构调研次数来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000970" code="000970">中科三环</a>机构调研最为密集，共获机构4次调研。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688662" code="688662">富信科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300286" code="300286">安科瑞</a>等机构调研也较为密集，均被机构调研2次。</p><p>20家以上机构扎堆调研股中，近5日资金净流入的有14只，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000762" code="000762">西藏矿业</a>近5日净流入资金10.48亿元，主力资金净流入最多；净流入资金较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000810" code="000810">创维数字</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600900" code="600900">长江电力</a>等。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202201/W020220107331329566028.png" title="近5日机构调研股一览.png" alt="近5日机构调研股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220107331329566028.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
