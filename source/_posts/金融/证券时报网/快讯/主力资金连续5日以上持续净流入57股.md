<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，截至2月21日收盘，共有57只个股主力资金连续5个交易日以上持续净流入。连续流入天数最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601366" code="601366">利群股份</a>，已连续23个交易日净流入；连续净流入天数较多的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601028" code="601028">玉龙股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300390" code="300390">天华超净</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600048" code="600048">保利发展</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300559" code="300559">佳发教育</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600219" code="600219">南山铝业</a>，分别获主力资金连续14个、11个、8个、8个、7个交易日净流入。从主力资金净流入总规模来看，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600199" code="600199">金种子酒</a>获主力资金净流入金额最多，连续6天累计净流入11.43亿元。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220222315391190910.png" title="连续5日或以上主力资金净流入个股排名.png" alt="连续5日或以上主力资金净流入个股排名.png" oldsrc="W020220222315391190910.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
