<p>证券时报网讯，银河证券指出，从需求的维度看，欧洲电解铝生产与俄罗斯电解铝出口受阻，可能造成欧洲部分下游企业被迫转向中国进行电解铝或铝加工品采购，也将利好国内电解铝消费，叠加国内稳增长政策加持，铝需求预期有望边际提升。随着国内铝下游开工进入旺季库存开始去化，铝价有望继续强势上涨，建议关注电解铝板块<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000807" code="000807">云铝股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000933" code="000933">神火股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002532" code="002532">天山铝业</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS601600" code="601600">中国铝业</a>。</p>
