<p>证券时报网讯，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" code="600030">中信证券</a>指出，全球粮食作物价格高位，提升农民种植意愿，带动农资品需求上行。前期受农药社会库存过高，且产能过剩等因素影响，除部分灭生性除草剂外，农药行业普遍呈现价格传导不通畅，企业营利性减弱等情况。9月中旬以来，受“双限”等因素影响，行业开工率减弱，叠加下游旺盛需求带来的社会库存减少及上游原材料价格高位所形成支撑，农药价格开始普涨，预期农资企业盈利能力有望持续改善。建议顺势而为，布局各细分农资赛道龙头企业，重点推荐国内菊酯龙头<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600486" code="600486">扬农化工</a>，草铵膦龙头<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002258" code="002258">利尔化学</a>，代森锰锌龙头<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002734" code="002734">利民股份</a>及积极布局海外市场的<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ301035" code="301035">润丰股份</a>，建议关注光气产业链较为完善的<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603599" code="603599">广信股份</a>以及精细农药化工品企业<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300575" code="300575">中旗股份</a>。</p>
