<p>证券时报网讯，据Choice统计显示，截止3月16日，2021年四季度末社保基金共现身27只个股前十大流通股东榜。其中，新进10只，增持7只。社保基金重仓股中，从前十大流通股东名单中社保基金家数来看，共有6只股获2家社保基金集中现身，持股量居前的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000708" code="000708">中信特钢</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600309" code="600309">万华化学</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300496" code="300496">中科创达</a>。</p><p>从持股比例看，社保基金持有比例最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ003038" code="003038">鑫铂股份</a>，持股量占流通股比例为7.61%；持股比例居前的还有<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300083" code="300083">创世纪</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300496" code="300496">中科创达</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000708" code="000708">中信特钢</a>等。持股数量方面，社保基金持股数量在1000万股以上的共有16只，社保基金持股量最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300083" code="300083">创世纪</a>，共持有7450.03万股；<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000001" code="000001">平安银行</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000708" code="000708">中信特钢</a>等紧随其后。</p>
