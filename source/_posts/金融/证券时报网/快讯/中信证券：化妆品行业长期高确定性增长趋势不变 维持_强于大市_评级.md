<p>证券时报e公司讯，<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600030" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600030" target="_blank">中信证券</a>研报认为，头部主播税收事件对化妆品国际品牌、本土传统品牌、互联网新锐品牌等均会带来阵痛，也将驱动化妆品生态圈里的各环节深刻变革，预计分化将进一步加剧，对A股相关上市公司利大于弊。综合本轮事件的影响和年末的估值切换，首推头部主播占比低、业绩增长确定性强的<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300957" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300957" target="_blank">贝泰妮</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603605" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="603605" target="_blank">珀莱雅</a>；其次为中长期成长空间广阔、强产品力的<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS688363" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="688363" target="_blank">华熙生物</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600223" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="600223" target="_blank">鲁商发展</a>；推荐化妆品产业链中上游的<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300856" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300856" target="_blank">科思股份</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300792" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" code="300792" target="_blank">壹网壹创</a>。化妆品行业长期高确定性增长趋势不变，维持“强于大市”评级。</p>
