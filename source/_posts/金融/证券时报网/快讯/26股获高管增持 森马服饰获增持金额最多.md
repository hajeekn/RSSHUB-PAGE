<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计显示，近10个交易日（2月9日~2月22日）共有26家上市公司股份获高管增持。从增持金额看，近10日增持金额最多的是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002563" code="002563">森马服饰</a>，累计增持538.82万股，增持金额合计3615.48万元；其次是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" code="300124">汇川技术</a>，增持量为38.22万股，增持金额2280.83万元。</p><p>增持次数方面，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002640" code="002640">*ST跨境</a>增持最频繁，近10日共有19条增持记录，<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300015" code="300015">爱尔眼科</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002778" code="002778">中晟高科</a>等紧随其后，近10日分别有8条、5条增持记录。<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ001215" code="001215">千味央厨</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ300124" code="300124">汇川技术</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="https://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS600221" code="600221">*ST海航</a>等期间也有两条以上的增持记录。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202202/W020220223362472118658.png" title="近10日高管增持股一览.png" alt="近10日高管增持股一览.png" oldsrc="W020220223362472118658.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
