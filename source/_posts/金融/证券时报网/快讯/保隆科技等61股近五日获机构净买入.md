<p>证券时报网讯，数据宝统计，近五个交易日的龙虎榜当中，100只个股出现了机构的身影，其中61只股票呈现机构净买入，39只股票呈现机构净卖出。近五日机构净买入前三名分别是<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SS603197" code="603197">保隆科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002326" code="002326">永太科技</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002459" code="002459">晶澳科技</a>。</p><p>与此同时，近五个交易日登陆沪深两市的龙虎榜个股中，营业部净买入较大的个股有海力风电、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ000723" code="000723">美锦能源</a>、<a class="trsHotWords" style="color: #2f67d1;text-decoration: none;" target="_blank" href="http://info.stcn.com/dc/stock/?stockcode=SZ002617" code="002617">露笑科技</a>。具体来看，海力风电是资金最青睐个股，营业部净买入金额达4.85亿元，高居榜首。</p><p style="text-align: center;"><img src="https://kuaixun.stcn.com/cj/202111/W020211126402460692551.png" title="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入的个股.png" alt="近五日龙虎榜机构净买入的个股.png" width="550" oldsrc="W020211126402460692551.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
