        <p><span style="font-weight: bold;">每经AI快讯，</span><span>7月15日，共有263只个股上榜龙虎榜，浪潮信息龙虎榜净买入额最多，达5.97亿元。</span></p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px; text-align: center;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/050dd45c33de47859498c65ba44a0f99?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及机构专用席位的个股有27只，净买入额前三的是浪潮信息、金城医药、吉比特，分别为2.86亿元、1.23亿元、1.23亿元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/2b9042044e7f4e0db36130b2a53491ee?nbddata-height=1146&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及沪股通专用席位的个股有3只，新凤鸣的沪股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入5373.37万元。</p>
<p style="line-height: 1.6; margin-bottom: 15px;"><img src="https://nbd-writer-1252627319.cos.ap-shanghai.myqcloud.com/67ed9850332c45fa8b5e059b9350d816?nbddata-height=498&nbddata-width=900" alt width="100%" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p>在龙虎榜中，涉及深股通专用席位的个股有9只，太钢不锈的深股通专用席位净买入额最大，净买入1.65亿元。</p>
