<div>   
<p>　　原标题 全国最大地下水“大漏斗”，修复得咋样了？</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220323s/87/w1080h607/20220323/2125-7815dfbe9e12e8e552022b4778a2896b.jpg" alt="农民在河北辛集市马兰农场节水小麦田里劳作" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">农民在河北辛集市马兰农场节水小麦田里劳作</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　你知道吗？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　全国最大地下水“大漏斗”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在华北</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　华北最大的地下水漏斗区</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在河北</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　过去数十年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　缺水的河北省</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　依靠超采地下水支撑经济社会发展</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　形成了近7万平方公里地下水漏斗区</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　引发了湿地萎缩、地面沉降等问题</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">如何治理“大漏斗”？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">这是摆在人们面前的一道难题</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河北出了个组合拳——</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“节、引、调、补、蓄、管”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">拧紧“龙头” </font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">节约用水</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在河北</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　75%的地下水用于农业灌溉</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　治理地下水“大漏斗”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　首先要拧紧“水龙头”节水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　马兰农场培育的系列节水小麦品种</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　开春后只浇一两水就能稳产</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　推广遍及河北 河南 山东 山西等地</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　累计覆盖3500多万亩</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　助力节水超125亿立方米</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220323s/88/w1080h608/20220323/e17e-7a0763f37a7babe55150c5aa05287f9d.jpg" alt="河北宁晋县高效农田示范区内的麦田进行节水浇灌" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">河北宁晋县高效农田示范区内的麦田进行节水浇灌</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　喷灌、滴灌等新技术</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　代替了传统“大水漫灌”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　实行季节性休耕</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　推广旱作雨养模式和抗旱节水品种</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　……</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220323s/93/w1080h613/20220323/45e4-e42f50cc154431b3484fea2a741a8a5e.jpg" alt="德龙钢铁公司水处理中心" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">德龙钢铁公司水处理中心</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　截至2021年底</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　累计关停取水井13万多眼</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一系列举措在河北推广</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　推进了农业节水增效</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　工业节水减排</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　城镇节水降损</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">置换水源</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">休养生息</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河北人均水资源量</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　仅为全国平均水平的1/7</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　曾经，也是农村饮水氟超标问题</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　严重省份</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不少村民长期喝高氟水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　即使是这样</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　地下水也越采越少</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　井越打越深</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　南水北调，调来了甘霖</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2014年底</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　南水北调中线通水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河北、天津、北京等受水区</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　迎来源源不断的长江水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在河北许多地区</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　南水成为主力水源</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　地下水应急备用</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一大批抽水井“下岗”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　地下水源进入“休养生息”状态</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220323s/200/w1080h720/20220323/b015-bde069f143bb7b88c23293ea88e02561.jpg" alt="南水北调中线配套工程廊坊市地表水厂" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">南水北调中线配套工程廊坊市地表水厂</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　利用通南水契机</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河北大力实施</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　受水区农村居民生活水源置换工程</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　农村居民生活水源</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　由当地的地下水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　置换为南水北调工程引来的长江水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　到今年底</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河北南水北调受水区</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2872万农村人口</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　将吃上长江水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　全省南水北调中线工程受益人口</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　达3550多万</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">生态补水</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">复苏河湖</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河渠、湖泊生态补水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是弥补地下水“亏空”直接有效途径</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220323s/290/w1080h810/20220323/0770-c1e54567268ca17740a1f0095739f7fb.jpg" alt="河北省固安县段的永定河" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">河北省固安县段的永定河</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　山西册田水库开闸</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　永定河春季补水全面启动</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　865公里长的河道</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今年将全线通水100天</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　补水量达7.92亿立方米</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　永定河又流动了起来</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　成为京津冀绿色生态廊道</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　和地下水补水通道</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　在20世纪</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　曾多次“干淀”的“华北明珠”白洋淀</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也因生态补水焕发迷人风采</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河北统筹黄河水、长江水和水库水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　向白洋淀实施生态补水</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　淀区面积由170多平方公里</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　恢复至275平方公里左右</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　水位稳定在7米左右</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“华北之肾”功能恢复</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220323s/88/w1080h608/20220323/9ec9-f994b8ee09f49b17c89af9c846b93ae5.jpg" alt="滹沱河石家庄市区段景色" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr">滹沱河石家庄市区段景色</span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河北省水利厅数据显示</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　近年来</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河北向70多条河道补水140多亿立方米</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沿线地下水得到回补</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　去年雨季</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　衡水市引蓄洪沥水2.4亿多立方米</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　约2800个坑塘“沟满壕平”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　区域地下水有效回补</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　实现人水和谐</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是绿色发展的不懈追求</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　截至2021年底</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河北累计压减地下水超采量</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　52.3亿立方米</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今年底</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　河北将提前完成</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　到2035年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　压减59.7亿立方米的治理任务</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">绿水青山</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">正在焕发更大光彩</font></p>
