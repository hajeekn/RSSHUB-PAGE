<div>   
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L color210">丨</font><font cms-style="font-L">来源：中国铁路微信</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　下面这篇攻略</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">送给即将乘车返程的你</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">出发前，检查下行李</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　有些在生活中常见的物品</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不能携带上火车</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如酒精、儿童玩具枪</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　超过120毫升的压力罐物品</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　超过20毫升的指甲油等</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/116/w550h366/20220209/0f9d-f20f685296b911d303ad518f2f09e0dc.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　出行前一定要仔细检查行李</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　确认是否有禁止或限制携带的物品</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如果您无法确定是否违规</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　可以在<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">铁路12306APP</font></font>上查询</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　也可拨打<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">12306铁路客服电话</font></font>咨询</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/337/w550h587/20220209/770b-9dac13d619c14a071aeb100c764f38db.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/318/w550h568/20220209/2d32-11c6442a5da123645db023616f402ce9.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">温馨提示：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　除导盲犬外，所有活体动物都不可以携带上车！</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">忘带身份证怎么办？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　匆匆赶到火车站</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　发现身份证丢失或忘带</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　怎么办？</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　手把手教你申请</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">电子临时身份证明</font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220209/d893-e3c306f3d47d83a60f5a692afd39e995.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　打开<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">铁路12306APP</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　点击“更多”找到“温馨服务”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　再点击“申请临时身份证明”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如实填写身份信息</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　进行刷脸认证</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">获得一个二维码</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这就是你的“临时身份证明”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/136/w550h386/20220209/afc7-738a5dfdd3d295f2e6ba5eac7520c63b.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/38/w550h288/20220209/6309-5084627cd75d1a198723d1b9073e331b.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/481/w550h731/20220209/d00f-7ae703056f2d2f5ed94af199796d2443.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">温馨提示：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　电子临时乘车身份证明24小时内有效，超过24小时需要重新申请办理，每名旅客在一个自然月内最多可申请3次。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">没赶上火车怎么办？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如果因为误车等原因</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　需要改变旅行计划</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">您可以办理一次车票改签</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　改签业务可以在</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">车站指定窗口、自动售票机</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">或铁路12306APP</font></font>等途径办理</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220209/dd6e-145677d1788720b9e2832de6836f6404.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/266/w550h516/20220209/f040-456fd4deab2efd7bb14ae7af702a35f3.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220209/d4ae-7a0de18d8ab4b59f5ecc75dda416a799.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">温馨提示：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　一张车票可以办理一次改签。车票改签后，如果您再次改变行程，可以按规定退票，但开车后改签的车票不能退票。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">中转换乘前一定要知道！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　需要中转换乘的旅客</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　下车后在站台墙壁或地面</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">找到“便捷换乘”标识</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　跟着引导标识进入换乘通道</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　可从站台直接到达候车区</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/116/w550h366/20220209/86b2-9db666dfb986341649866b2d62d8d7c4.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　进入便捷通道前</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　车站工作人员需要核验</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　旅客换乘车票信息</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　请您提前准备好</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　购票时使用的有效身份证件</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="11" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/116/w550h366/20220209/1a7f-59ee348ac97196183aebd5faed585dc1.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">温馨提示：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　如果您找不到相应的标识，可以询问站台工作人员。受各地疫情影响，便捷换乘通道将会动态调整开启时间。</p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">东西落在火车上怎么办？</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如果您将行李物品</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　遗落在列车上或车站里</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　您可以及时向铁路工作人员反映</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　或者<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">拨打铁路12306服务电话</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">讲清遗失物品的具体信息</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　铁路部门会全力为您找寻</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="12" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220209/32b1-de1309b5017adc2d408c1ab3b7831dc6.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　另外，铁路12306APP设有</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“遗失物品查找”服务功能</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　你按服务单内容逐项填写完整后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　可随时关注报失进度查询</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="13" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/777/w550h1027/20220209/bf18-82358fd78b9a3cf4d9f217ac5dc01d7f.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold">温馨提示：</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　出门在外一定要看管好自己的行李物品，特别是贵重物品哦！</p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock, -->
