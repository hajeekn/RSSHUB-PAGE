<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　隆冬腊月，数九寒天</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　街头车流滚滚，熙熙攘攘；</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　工厂热火朝天，忙碌一片</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　滴水成冰，热气腾腾</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">我是沈阳</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">一座在共和国工业版图中</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">举足轻重的工业重镇</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">一座不惧看衰与质疑</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">锚定振兴目标奋力前行的“振兴之城”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">一座初露“靓美”身形</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">一点点蜕变的工业新都</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　新年伊始，来我这里</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　体验“老字号”的“香”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　感受“新字号”的“势”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　欣赏“靓身形”的“美”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">看！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">不可替代的“老字号”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">历久弥“香”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　走过七十载的沈阳鼓风机集团</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　生产有“工业心脏”之称的压缩机</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220119/16c1-ecae1130c657756a7614ff7195ccc73e.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L">　　在沈阳鼓风机集团车间，工人操作机器加工产品内机壳（2020年3月16日摄）。新华社记者 杨青 摄 </p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　它多年来始终坚持</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　研发投入占总收入的6%以上 </p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是沈阳装备制造业的代表</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今年刚一开年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沈鼓的厂房车间又是忙得不可开交</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　不可替代</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　所以订单满满</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2021年1至11月</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">集团营业收入、利润总额</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">分别同比增长16.5%、3.4倍</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">2022年的订单已排到了下半年</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　从失血到止血</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一度濒临破产的沈阳机床备受外界关注</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“现在我们是一个人顶俩人用。”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沈阳机床中捷友谊厂总经理赵旭靖说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　通用技术集团2019年底重组沈阳机床后</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　力推三项制度改革</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">人均产值由37.8万元提高到87.9万元</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　改革进一步，效益长一寸</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　通过混合所有制改革的北方重工</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">2021年营业收入、新增订货</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">分别同比增长442.8%和427.1%</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“老”不是落后的标签</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">而是支撑振兴的根基</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　初步统计</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沈阳市2021年地区生产总值同比增长7%左右</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　其中规模以上工业增加值同比增长9%左右</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">看！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">跳跃前行的“新字号”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">跃跃欲“势”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　机器手臂上下舞动</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　上演着<span id="stock_sz300024"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300024/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">机器人</a></span><span id="quote_sz300024"></span>生产的画面</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　增强现实等技术与传统工业接入</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　构画出“科幻大片”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　5G基站雨后春笋般出现在车间厂矿周围</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　标识解析二级节点</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“星火·链网”超级节点</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一个个工业的新名词在这里落地生根</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　行走在沈阳</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　能深刻感受到汩汩涌动的新动能</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/117/w550h367/20220119/abcb-c274444232b600cbfa0b1eef23ffeeab.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2021年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沈阳市瞪羚、独角兽企业同比增长71.59%</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“新势力”正在加速崛起成“势”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　走进新时代的沈阳</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　展现出了对“新”前所未有的渴望</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　这，孕育着新的未来</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">看！</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color210">正在蜕变的“靓身形”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center color210">　　初具轮廓</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　流经沈阳的浑河北岸</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　每到夜晚</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　盛京大剧院就会发出绚丽的光彩</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　音乐会、京剧、儿童剧</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　经常一票难求</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　1905文创园</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　奉天工厂</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　铁锚1956文创园</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　老工厂接二连三变身</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　成为年轻人的“网红”打卡地</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如今的沈阳</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　已经不再是刻板印象中的“钢筋铁骨”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">这里“国际范”更浓</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　中德园、中日园、中韩园</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　……</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/60/w550h310/20220119/ebdd-64ddd1177d0bd74551e93efbf4f93d6b.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　随着一个个中外合作工业园的成熟</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　大街小巷也多了很多洋面孔</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就在2021年底</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沈阳还举办了一场主题晚会</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　来自20多个国家的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　100多名旅居沈阳的外国友人登台献艺</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">这里“数字味”更强</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　刷脸功能在地铁商超中应用</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　足不出户的“一网通办”全面推进</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　智慧城市应用场景加快布局</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沈阳正在把办事大厅“放到百姓的指尖上”</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　把城市管理架构在数据上</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">这里“低碳风”正劲</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　2021年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　沈阳空气质量优良天数达到315天</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　首次突破了300天</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一块块光伏发电板</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一根根风力发电桩</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一座座家门口的口袋公园</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　如今的沈阳</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　绿色已经成了新发展底色</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/finance/crawl/60/w550h310/20220119/5b46-0d9d74d8b45dcdf380801396db9b4b09.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　走过深冬，便是新春</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　滚石上山的振兴之路</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　山险石巨</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但春山在望，未来可期</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">我的“老”，是振兴的根基</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">我的“新”，是振兴的希望</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">我的“靓”，是振兴的彰显</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">我是沈阳</font></p>
