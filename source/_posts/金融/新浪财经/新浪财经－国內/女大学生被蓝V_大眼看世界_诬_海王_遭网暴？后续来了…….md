<p>　　原标题：女大学生被诬“海王”遭网暴？后续来了……</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　11月11日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">在北京一所大学读大四</font>的</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">博主小嘉（化名）的维权帖</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　引发关注</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="0" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211116s/307/w1080h827/20211116/964f-6ee66fe99d4fd3f0c69f079ea419111e.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　就在10日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一短视频账号将她的照片</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">和毫无关联的网络传言拼凑在一起</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　做成短视频在多个网络平台上发布</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">点赞迅速破万</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="1" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211116s/190/w439h551/20211116/7a3c-09563bf3338c7e840cd0c1c769ed43a4.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">有不明真相的人</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">看到造谣视频后</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">用跟贴和私信等方式对她进行谩骂攻击</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　她决心拿起法律武器</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　维护自己的权益</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">她说</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“这次事件不仅是为自己发声</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">也是为被谩骂和被造谣的人发声”</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211116s/663/w1080h1183/20211116/a039-bfc4aba75f6626a09f12b4b1d6635393.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　值得注意的是</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　发布该短视频的账号</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是一个名为“大眼看世界”的蓝V</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　视频<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">用的文字材料是传言</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">照片却是小嘉的美妆照</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">小嘉录制的澄清视频表示</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">文字信息写的是长春的一名女生</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">而她是湖南人，在北京读书</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">短视频称小嘉在花呗有欠款</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　但是她从来没有开通过花呗</p>
<div class="img_wrapper">
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211116s/374/w1080h1694/20211116/43e6-a1f53211842867c6338e15cafee9eee7.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
</div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　更让小嘉愤怒和震惊的是——</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　“仅凭四张图片就能把你写得如此不堪</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　一些不明真相的网友</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">不仅在评论区对我说了很难听的话</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">甚至还攻击我的父母”</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="4" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211116s/119/w1080h1439/20211116/74c7-a79c23497e99a87831b571dcbc473508.jpg" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　15日</p>
<p cms-style="font-L  align-Center">　　该账号发表《致歉信》</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　并表示<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">永久关停账号</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　目前，账号视频内容已全部清空</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="5" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211116s/410/w1080h930/20211116/3122-ad840b159cf88a8483fd224e29898802.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　维权一事得到不少网友的支援</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　大家纷纷表示</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">别让伤害继续传递</font></font></p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="6" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211116s/446/w1080h166/20211116/f444-9570f43f9edd8097d1886441d041f7dc.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="7" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211116s/486/w1080h206/20211116/4028-a480784f931d12e9f2024b3703aa5da1.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="8" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211116s/463/w1080h183/20211116/9df7-d4d744df3cdeac796bd706c29d5c0a98.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="9" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211116s/538/w1080h258/20211116/a75e-7a1f2efd7e20db21e56e721b4eccd09f.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="10" src="https://n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20211116s/451/w1080h171/20211116/8b1d-c73e2f24b67d8216d212683d0700d111.png" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">不造谣、不传谣</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">理性看待热点</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">坚决对网络暴力说不！</font></font></p>
