<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">从网友们的讨论来看，</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">甚至在会议上或工作群里大肆宣传，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这样的工作方式，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">已经变了味道。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">央视网也曾评论</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“崇尚奋斗不等于‘美化加班’”。</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">对于管理者而言，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">适当控制自己对工作的“热情”，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">给员工一些私人时间；</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">而对于员工而言，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">工作时间认真完成好工作，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">下班时间多陪陪家人，</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">这才是良性的生活状态。</font></font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">不要把变相的“剥削”当成“奋斗”，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">避免工作上的“内卷”，</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">这才是“大家好才是真的好”。</font></p>
