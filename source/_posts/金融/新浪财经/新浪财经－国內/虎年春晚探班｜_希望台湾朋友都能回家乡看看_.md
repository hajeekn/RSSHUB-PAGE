<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　虎年春晚的脚步越来越近<!--article_adlist[<img src="https://mmbiz.qpic.cn/mmbiz_png/oq1PymRl9D7yjBrrlRK4fHeNjWkicWIFhKN7BuPEgj1tyFjjtTGOpsGlQxVVYnZyBGrDCElNgGHZfzurfZjiaNiaA/640?wx_fmt=png" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="20" cms-height="20" black-list="y"/>]article_adlist--></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　彩排间隙，多名演职人员</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">接受了</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">央视</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color30"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color30">新</font></font></font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">闻</font></font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">的采访</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><!--article_adlist[<img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220129s/102/w51h51/20220129/f887-88a10c21968ce7f79cfb78adaed1fbf7.gif" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="30" cms-height="30"/>]article_adlist--></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center strong-Bold">　　萧敬腾带新歌上春晚</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“希望台湾朋友都能回家乡看看”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">歌手萧敬腾</font>曾多次参加春晚</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　他说，每次站上春晚舞台</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　陪大家一起过年</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　是一件很幸福、很骄傲的事情</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“希望大家都能够感受到</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">我们发自内心的祝福”</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“想先不透露</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">但是会给大家一个</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　谈到自己的搭档，邓超说</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　两位都是自己非常想合作的老师</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“开始（我）是压力巨大的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">后来发现从他们身上</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">能学到很多东西”</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center"><!--article_adlist[<img src="//n.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220129s/102/w51h51/20220129/f887-88a10c21968ce7f79cfb78adaed1fbf7.gif" style="max-width:640px" cms-width="30" cms-height="30"/>]article_adlist--></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">再登春晚，他们说</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center">“春晚就像老朋友”</font></p>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">春晚就像是一个</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“老朋友”</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">大家都是在这样一个夜晚</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">与家人团圆、</font><font cms-style="font-L align-Center">辞旧迎新</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　迎接<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">美好的</font>新年”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="2" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://f.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220129s/184/w632h352/20220129/cbc6-5d4fc5f98223dda94e0c6c32c589b23c.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>

<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　自己和春晚非常有缘</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L align-Center">“</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">春晚是1983年创办的</font></font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">我是1983年生的</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　第二次来到春晚的舞台，非常开心”</p>
<div class="img_wrapper"><img id="3" style="max-width: 640px;" src="https://f.sinaimg.cn/sinakd20220129s/747/w480h267/20220129/2b2f-ce80ac2de4a0480f6bea60658b32ec4b.gif" alt referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><span class="img_descr"></span></div>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　今年，郁可唯将和赵丽颖、朱一龙、王俊凯</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　四人同台演绎一首歌曲</p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">“这首歌有一种</font></p>
<p cms-style="font-L align-Center">　　<font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0"><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">回望过去、</font><font cms-style="font-L strong-Bold align-Center color0">展望美好的未来</font></font></p>
