<p>　　原标题：突发！网传毛阿敏老公、“中植系”掌门人解直锟离世 控制8家A股上市公司</p>
<p>　　北京商报讯（记者 马换换）12月18日，据财新报道，“中植系”掌门人解直锟离世，享年60岁。</p>
<p>　　解直锟系A股大佬，Wind统计显示，解直锟控制的上市公司有<span id="stock_sz002012"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002012/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">凯恩股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002012"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002621"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002621/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">美吉姆</a></span><span id="quote_sz002621"></span>、<span id="stock_sh600107"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sh600107/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">美尔雅</a></span><span id="quote_sh600107"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002289"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002289/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">宇顺电子</a></span><span id="quote_sz002289"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002207"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002207/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">准油股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002207"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002418"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002418/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">康盛股份</a></span><span id="quote_sz002418"></span>、<span id="stock_sz002622"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz002622/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">融钰集团</a></span><span id="quote_sz002622"></span>，今年下半年还拿下<span id="stock_sz300313"><a href="https://finance.sina.com.cn/realstock/company/sz300313/nc.shtml" class="keyword" target="_blank" data-sudaclick="content_marketkeywords_p">ST天山</a></span><span id="quote_sz300313"></span>的控制权。</p>
<p>　　据了解，解直锟，1961年生人，中国改革开放的第一代企业家，于1995年创立中植集团。</p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock,sz002012&sz002207&sz002289&sz002418&sz002621&sz002622&sz300313&sh600107 -->
