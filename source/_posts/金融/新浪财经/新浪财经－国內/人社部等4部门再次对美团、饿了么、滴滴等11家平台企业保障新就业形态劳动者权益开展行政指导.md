<p>　　<strong>人力资源社会保障部等4部门再次对美团、饿了么、滴滴等11家平台企业保障新就业形态劳动者权益开展行政指导</strong></p>
<p>　　<span style="line-height: 32px;">日前</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">，人力资源社会保障部会同</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">交通运输部</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">、市场监管总局</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">、</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">全国总工会</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">再次</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">就</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">维护</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">新就业形态劳动者</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">劳动保障</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">权益</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">对</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">美团、饿了么、滴滴、</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">曹操、</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">达达、闪送、货拉拉、满帮、</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">到家集团</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">、阿里巴巴、腾讯</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">等</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">1</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">1</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">家</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">头部平台企业</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">联合开展</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">行政指导</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">。</span></p>
<p>　　<span style="line-height: 32px;">会议</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">肯定了</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">头部平台企业贯彻落实《关于维护新就业形态劳动者劳动保障权益的指导意见》等政策</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">取得</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">的阶段性成效，要求头部平台企业进一步提高保障好新就业形态劳动者权益的</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">政治自觉、</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">思想自觉</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">和行动自觉</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">，</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">深刻</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">认识党中央、国务院维护新就业形态劳动者权益的坚定决心、全</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">社会对平台企业</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">维护新就业形态劳动者权益</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">的殷切期望和新就业形态劳动者的迫切期待。</span></p>
<p>　　<span style="line-height: 32px;">会议</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">强调</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">，平台企业要</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">全面</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">准确理解政策要求，在前期工作基础上，</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">深入了解</span><span style="line-height: 32px;">劳动者诉求，加大对用工合作企业保障劳动者权益的监督力度，持续完善平台算法和劳动规则，健全企业保障新就业形态劳动者权益的制度机制。</span></p><!-- news_keyword_pub,stock, -->
