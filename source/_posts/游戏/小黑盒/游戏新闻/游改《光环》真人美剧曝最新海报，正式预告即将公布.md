<p>日前，根据射击游戏《光环》改编的真人美剧官方推特发布了新的海报，并且宣布剧集首款预告将于本周日正式发布。剧集将于2022年上线流媒体Paramount+。《光环》是由斯比尔伯格担当监制的真人剧集，由Amblin Entertainment公司出品。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="747" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/01/26/99335c284c194070bbd0b3e92910ceb61643185923.png" data-width="602" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>剧集将讲述26世纪人类与外星力量“星盟”的史诗级冲突，26世纪最先进的战士“士官长”将由巴勃罗·施瑞博尔饰演，他也是被推到毁灭边缘的文明得救的唯一希望。娜塔莎· 麦克艾霍恩、夏莉·墨菲、莎巴娜·阿兹米、博基姆·伍德拜因、Yerin Ha等也参演。感兴趣的观众们敬请期待。</p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="515" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/01/26/3a7b90d123f346e99f0d83062f6567c61643185924.png" data-width="870" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="514" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/01/26/3fd04d08e9a14d91a98a27531028840d1643185925.png" data-width="922" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><br></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="513" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/01/26/a5b10c5d01d54acab046fbb9d6b126d81643185925.png" data-width="999" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="578" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/01/26/385f4008a6bc4424a80516a448cfca5e1643185926.png" data-width="1002" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img class="lazy" data-height="529" src="https://i1.max-c.com/imgx/2022/01/26/1ff46d8073814285894f892f20cf9dbf1643185927.png" data-width="1013" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><blockquote><p>来源：游侠网</p></blockquote>
