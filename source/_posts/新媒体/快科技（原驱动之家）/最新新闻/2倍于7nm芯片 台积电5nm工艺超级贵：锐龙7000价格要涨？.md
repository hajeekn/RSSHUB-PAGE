<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年会有更多的厂商进入<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>节点，除了苹果之外，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>今年推出的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>处理器也会升级台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>，具体产品就是锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>，最快<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月份之前就上市了，不过这一代处理器的成本大增，因为<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>代工价格实在是太贵。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据之前乔治敦大学沃尔什外交学院安全与新兴技术中心（<span lang="EN-US">CSET</span>）发布的一篇报告，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>台积电<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺代工的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>英寸晶圆价格要<span lang="EN-US">9300</span>美元左右，<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺代工价格则要<span lang="EN-US">17000</span>美元左右，<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺将进一步增加到<span lang="EN-US">30000</span>美元。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>升级到了<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，理论上成本增加将近一倍，而且这还只是生产上的成本，如果考虑到先进工艺的设计费用，<span lang="EN-US">Semiengingeering</span>之前公布的报告称<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>设计费就要<span lang="EN-US">2.97</span>亿美元，<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>更是增加到<span lang="EN-US">5.4</span>亿美元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">如果再考虑到最近一年多芯片制造行业的各种原材料涨价，那么<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺生产新一代锐龙处理器的成本显然要增加很多，下游产品涨价是不可避免的。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>发布<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5000</span>处理器时，<strong><span lang="EN-US">6</span>核的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">5 5600X</span>都要<span lang="EN-US">2099</span>元，引发玩家不满，</strong>今年的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>上了<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，如何定价都要考验<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的水平了。</p>
