<p class="MsoNormal">在全球半导体公司中，荷兰的<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>公司规模不算顶尖，但它们掌握了主要的光刻机市场，<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机买到<span lang="EN-US">10</span>亿一台都供不应求，业绩红到发紫，这也让<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>管理层拿奖到手软，<span lang="EN-US">CEO</span>去年就拿到<span lang="EN-US">3500</span>万元的奖励。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>公司发布的信息，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年中公司管理层获得了超过<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>万欧元的奖励，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>其中最多的是<span lang="EN-US">CEO Peter Wennink</span>和<span lang="EN-US">CTO Martin van de Brink</span>，每人拿到了<span lang="EN-US">480</span>万欧元的薪酬， 比上一年多了<span lang="EN-US">30</span>万欧元，总计约合<span lang="EN-US">3500</span>万人民币。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">CFO Roger Dassen</span>去年的薪酬是<span lang="EN-US">380</span>万欧元，与上一年持平，还有其他两位高管分别获得了<span lang="EN-US">310</span>万欧元的奖励，比之前增加了<span lang="EN-US">20</span>万欧元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">管理层获得重奖跟去年的业绩有关，根据<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>的数据，<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>公司实现净销售额<span lang="EN-US">186.11</span>亿欧元，较上一财年增长<span lang="EN-US">33%</span>，净利润也从<span lang="EN-US">35.54</span>亿欧元涨至<span lang="EN-US">58.83</span>亿欧元，全年净订单额达到<span lang="EN-US">262.4</span>亿欧元，较去年同期翻了一倍，需求强烈。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年中，<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>出货了<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机总计多达<span lang="EN-US">42</span>套，<strong><span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年是<span lang="EN-US">31</span>套，实现营收<span lang="EN-US">63</span>亿美元，算下来平均每套<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机要<span lang="EN-US">1.5</span>亿欧元，折合人民币<span lang="EN-US">10.8</span>亿元。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当然，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>亿一台的<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机还不是最贵的，新一代<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机也开始产生订单了，据说成本高达<span lang="EN-US">3</span>亿美元，约合<span lang="EN-US">19</span>亿元。</p>
