<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">除了<span lang="EN-US">AX6</span>单体路由器之外，华为刚刚还发布了子母路由器<span lang="EN-US">Q6</span>，专为大户型设计，<span lang="EN-US">150</span>平米以上的大别墅也能全屋覆盖<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6+</span>网络，速率<span lang="EN-US">3000Mbps</span>，还支持鸿蒙<span lang="EN-US">HarmonyOS</span>智慧网络。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">子母路由器<span lang="EN-US">Q6</span>最多支持<span lang="EN-US">1</span>带<span lang="EN-US">15</span>子路由，不论是<span lang="EN-US">120</span>平米大平层，<strong>还是<span lang="EN-US">150</span>平米以上别墅都可以全屋覆盖，支持<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6+</span>网络，速率可达<span lang="EN-US">3000Mbps</span>。</strong></p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220316/4b10892f-24d5-4994-b4e7-06267d9db215.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="华为发布Q6子母路由器：3Gbps网速+别墅级Wi-Fi 6+ 哪里信号不好插哪里" h="199" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220316/S4b10892f-24d5-4994-b4e7-06267d9db215.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">子母路由器<span lang="EN-US">Q6</span>采用<span lang="EN-US">PLC</span>电力线连接，支持华为首创的<span lang="EN-US">PLC Turbo 2.0</span>技术，速度比普通电力线快一倍，可达<span lang="EN-US">1300Mbps</span>，<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>抗干扰技术，稳定不掉线，有电就有<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi</span>，覆盖阳台、洗手间等多个位置。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此外，子母路由器<span lang="EN-US">Q6</span>还支持全屋统一<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi</span>名称，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>重漫游技术加持，并支持华为业界首创的三链路超级组网，支持<span lang="EN-US">2.4G+5G+</span>电力线组网，无惧穿墙。</p>


<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220316/437c57847f5448f98567d1dbd3219304.png" target="_blank"><img alt="华为发布Q6子母路由器：3Gbps网速+别墅级Wi-Fi 6+ 哪里信号不好插哪里" h="391" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220316/s_437c57847f5448f98567d1dbd3219304.png" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，子母路由器<span lang="EN-US">Q6</span>还支持<span lang="EN-US">HarmonyOS</span>智慧网络，轻松一碰就可以免密码联网，支持儿童模式，还支持智能手游加速，智能识别<span lang="EN-US">140</span>多款热门手游。</p>
