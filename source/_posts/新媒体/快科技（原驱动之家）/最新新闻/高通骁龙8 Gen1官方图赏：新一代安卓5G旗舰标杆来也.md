<p class="MsoNormal">今天早上高通在骁龙技术峰会上发布了骁龙<span lang="EN-US">8 Gen1</span>，这是高通最新一代移动平台，同时也是安卓阵营新一代旗舰的标杆，包括小米、<span lang="EN-US">vivo</span>、<span lang="EN-US">OPPO</span>等在内的<span lang="EN-US">14</span>家手机品牌都将采用骁龙<span lang="EN-US">8 Gen1</span>，本月就会有商用终端问世。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">骁龙<span lang="EN-US">8 Gen1</span>带来了更先进的<span lang="EN-US">5G</span>、<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>、游戏、影像和<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi</span>与蓝牙技术，采用三星<span lang="EN-US">4nm</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>部分采用<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个<span lang="EN-US">Cortex-X2</span>超大核，频率<span lang="EN-US">3.0GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个<span lang="EN-US">Cortex-A710</span>大核，频率<span lang="EN-US">2.5GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个<span lang="EN-US">Cortex-A510</span>小核，频率<span lang="EN-US">1.8GHz</span>，也就是全部基于<span lang="EN-US">ARM v9</span>纯<span lang="EN-US">64</span>位指令集打造的<span lang="EN-US">8</span>核<span lang="EN-US">Kryo</span>，<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>性能提升<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>。能耗最高减少<span lang="EN-US">30%</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，新一代<span lang="EN-US">Adreno GPU</span>性能提升<span lang="EN-US">30%</span>，能耗减少<span lang="EN-US">25%</span>，并且升级<span lang="EN-US">Snapdragon Sight</span>，带来了<span lang="EN-US">18bit</span>三路<span lang="EN-US">ISP</span>及<span lang="EN-US">8K HDR</span>视频拍摄等功能。</p>

<p style="text-align: center"><img alt="高通骁龙8 Gen1官方图赏：新一代安卓5G旗舰标杆来也" h="760" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20211201/b949a35a-305e-4be6-84b2-a8afd4463245.png" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">高通技术公司高级副总裁兼移动、计算及基础设施业务总经理阿力克斯·卡图赞表示：“作为全球最先进的移动平台，骁龙是顶级<span lang="EN-US">Android</span>体验的代名词，全新一代骁龙<span lang="EN-US">8</span>移动平台将为下一代旗舰移动终端树立新标杆，并为智能手机带来前所未有的连接、影像、<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>、游戏、音频和安全体验。<span lang="EN-US">”</span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">全球众多<span lang="EN-US">OEM</span>厂商和品牌，<strong>包括中兴通讯、小米、<span lang="EN-US">vivo</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Redmi</span>、<span lang="EN-US">realme</span>、<span lang="EN-US">OPPO</span>、一加、努比亚、<span lang="EN-US">iQOO</span>、荣耀、黑鲨、索尼、夏普以及<span lang="EN-US">Motorola</span>，将采用全新一代骁龙<span lang="EN-US">8</span>移动平台，商用终端预计将于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年底面市。</strong></p>
