<p class="MsoNormal">今年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月份初，<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>宣布放弃收购<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>公司，一年半之前他们还雄心壮志地要拿下<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>公司，随着股价暴涨，交易价格也从之前的<span lang="EN-US">400</span>亿美元涨到了<span lang="EN-US">660</span>多亿美元，折合人民币超过<span lang="EN-US">4100</span>多亿，差点就成为半导体行业史上最贵的收购案。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">由于收购没有完成，<strong><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>也不得不赔付了<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>公司一笔违约金，大约是<span lang="EN-US">12.5</span>亿美元，</strong>会对<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>的财务造成一些负面影响。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>对<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>收购失利一事并不怎么在乎，而且也不会影响他们跟<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>的友好关系，日前<span lang="EN-US">CFO</span>首席财务官<span lang="EN-US">Colette Kress</span>在也摩根斯坦利的大会上谈到了<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>与<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>的关系。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Colette Kress</span>表示<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>拥有开发<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>处理器十多年的经验，并且拿下了<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>指令授权，双方将继续富有成效的合作关系。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>也认可<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>的重要性，<span lang="EN-US">Colette Kress</span>称<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>依然是未来十年最重要的处理器指令之一。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至于<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>这边，他们之前开发的<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>处理器主要是用于嵌入式平台的，但是去年<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>发布了<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺的数据中心处理器<span lang="EN-US">Grace</span>，具体核心数量、频率、缓存、功耗等暂未公布，但是性能很强大。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">据NVIDIA介绍，Grace搭配<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5X</span>内存可提供超过<span lang="EN-US">500GB/s</span>的带宽，而且支持<span lang="EN-US">ECC</span>错误校验，<strong>通过缓存一致性<span lang="EN-US">NVLink</span>总线连接自家<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>，带宽可超过<span lang="EN-US">900GB/s</span>，同时两颗<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>之间的带宽超过<span lang="EN-US">600GB/s</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">该处理器预计在<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年问世，瑞士国家超级计算中心<span lang="EN-US">(CSCS)</span>、慧与<span lang="EN-US">(HPE)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>联合使用<span lang="EN-US">Grace CPU</span>、下一代<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA GPU</span>，正在共同打造号称世界最快<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>超算的<span lang="EN-US">“ALPS”(</span>阿尔卑斯山<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，算力高达<span lang="EN-US">20ExaFlops(</span>每秒<span lang="EN-US">2</span>千亿亿次计算<span lang="EN-US">)</span>，主要用于天气研究与预报、材料科学等。</p>
