<p class="MsoNormal">昨天全球最大的光刻机供应商<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>发布了<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>及全年财报，去年营收<span lang="EN-US">186.11</span>亿欧元，较上一财年增长<span lang="EN-US">33%</span>，净利润也从<span lang="EN-US">35.54</span>亿欧元涨至<span lang="EN-US">58.83</span>亿欧元，全年出货<span lang="EN-US">42</span>套<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机，比上一年增加<span lang="EN-US">11</span>套。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度中，<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>公司还新增了<span lang="EN-US">71</span>亿欧元的新订单，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/810/810304.htm" target="_blank">其中就有一套<span lang="EN-US">NA 0.55</span>高数值孔径<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机的销售</a>，这是<span lang="EN-US">ASML</span>下一代光刻机，订购这个光刻机的是<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>，据说成本高达<span lang="EN-US">3</span>亿美元，约合<span lang="EN-US">19</span>亿元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">要知道，在当前的<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机销售中，台积电及三星都是大客户，订单量远高于<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>但在下一代<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机中<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>拿到了独占权，有一定的抢先期，早在<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年就下第一台。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在当前<span lang="EN-US">NA 0.33</span>的<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机中，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>动作慢了，被台积电、三星抢先，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>工艺芯片量产要到<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年的<span lang="EN-US">14</span>代酷睿<span lang="EN-US">Metor Lake</span>上。</p>

<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-align: center;"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220120/a833d9c7-7dea-4aee-883b-31930b5ce24e.jpg" style="text-align: center;" target="_blank"><img alt="单价19亿元 Intel四年前就抢购下一代EUV光刻机：新工艺绝不落后" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220120/Sa833d9c7-7dea-4aee-883b-31930b5ce24e.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">在下一代<span lang="EN-US">NA 0.55</span>的<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻工艺中，<strong><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>不允许同样的错误再次发生，这一次要抢先三星台积电下单，也会抢先量产<span lang="EN-US">20A</span>及<span lang="EN-US">18A</span>工艺，首次进入埃米级工艺节点。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">基于此，<strong><span lang="EN-US">4</span>年来<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>实际上已经下单了<span lang="EN-US">6</span>台<span lang="EN-US">NA 0.55</span>的<span lang="EN-US">EUV</span>光刻机，</strong>其中分为两种，<span lang="EN-US">Twinscan Exe:5000</span>系列主要用于工艺研发，产能输出是<span lang="EN-US">185WPH</span>，每小时生产<span lang="EN-US">185</span>片晶圆，<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年上半年交付。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">量产型的<span lang="EN-US">NA 0.55</span>光刻机是<span lang="EN-US">Twinscan Exe:5200</span>，产能提升到<span lang="EN-US">200WPH</span>，每小时<span lang="EN-US">200</span>片晶圆，预计会在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年下线，<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的<span lang="EN-US">20A</span>工艺正好是在<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年量产。</p>
