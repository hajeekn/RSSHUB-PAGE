<p class="MsoNormal">今年初的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>展会上，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>正式官宣了<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>架构的锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>系列处理器，基于台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺，最新消息称<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>的进度比传闻的更快，今年五六月份就能发布，三季度就能上市。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>之后就要到<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>架构了，现在还没有出现在<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>官方路线图中，但是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>之前表示<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>已经在开发中了，按照一年一升级的惯例来看，<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>应该是在<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年上市。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前多个消息来源都声称，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong><span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>要比<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>更令人激动得多，可以类比<span lang="EN-US">Zen</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Zen2</span>之间的提升幅度，<span lang="EN-US">IPC</span>同频性能相比于<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>有望提升多达<span lang="EN-US">20-40</span>％。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">桌面版的<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>产品目前规划是包含<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>大核心、<span lang="EN-US">16</span>个<span lang="EN-US">Zen4D</span>小核心，总计<span lang="EN-US">24</span>核心，如果都支持多线程的话那就是<span lang="EN-US">48</span>线程。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>之前预期是升级台积电<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺，然而现在变数增加了，<strong>之前爆料这个消息的消息人士<span lang="EN-US">Greymon55</span>表示<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>有可能切换到<span lang="EN-US">4nm</span>工艺，类似的还有下下代<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>芯片<span lang="EN-US">RNDA4</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">导致这一结果的原因就是台积电的<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺不如预期，之前我们报道过台积电<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>工艺良率似乎还有问题，要到<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年初才能给苹果、<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>供货。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">考虑到<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>节点<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>也要占不少产能，重要性可能超过<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>，所以<span lang="EN-US">Zen5</span>处理器的<span lang="EN-US">3nm</span>产能真的会遇到一些麻烦。</p>
