<p>当下正值冬奥期间，广大网友也都在观看这一体育盛宴，好的赛事当然要用更高级的设备来观赏。如今什么样的手机最有高级感？</p>
<p class="newsinfo_vedio"><a class="mydrivers_player" href="https://weibo.com/tv/show/1034:4735356453716008">视频</a></p>
<p>曲面屏一定是首选，不过市面上质感不错的曲面屏手机还真的都不便宜。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/18d74793554f40be823e5f520c94aafa.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_18d74793554f40be823e5f520c94aafa.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>我手上拿着的这台荣耀60 SE就不一样了。曲面屏设计的它颜值非常高，但价格只要2000多元，在这个档位可以说找不到对手了。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/1c8cacf66c034872a06515ffcc0c872c.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_1c8cacf66c034872a06515ffcc0c872c.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>这块屏是荣耀60 SE最大的卖点。中置打孔、58°曲面的设计，边框非常窄，手机正面观感满满的一块屏，沉浸感很强。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/b8a723c587e64702bf637d945c6c75bb.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_b8a723c587e64702bf637d945c6c75bb.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>这块6.67寸的OLED屏还采用了新一代排列方式，支持120Hz刷新率和10.7亿色显示，并且全局支持1920Hz高频调光，可以减轻眼睛疲劳。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/807d1319b31d42dd9c7ee97636c166a9.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_807d1319b31d42dd9c7ee97636c166a9.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>它的后摄采用了类似iPhone的设计，三摄三角形排布，搭配矩形的模组和圆润的边角曲线，既有辨识度，整体也更加和谐。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/6fa6da9705cf4079a158070294ffd852.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_6fa6da9705cf4079a158070294ffd852.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>机身采用了3D玻璃材质，比塑料要有质感得多。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/1b281ce3a0e8446fbedf1f28b1020c2d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_1b281ce3a0e8446fbedf1f28b1020c2d.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>我收到的这款流光幻镜配色，在光照下，可以呈现两种不断变化的光纹，后摄模组和整体后盖反射的纹路还不尽相同，光影效果更富层次。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/c02f828951b74aba98b5b7e2d2a1feb8.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_c02f828951b74aba98b5b7e2d2a1feb8.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>荣耀60 SE本身就只有175g重，7.7mm厚，还采用了双曲面设计，进一步提升了握持手感。如果你受不了动辄200多克的半斤机，那么荣耀60 SE绝对是单手握持的福音。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/e8e47d0a6a874f9ea632a4487a366de9.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_e8e47d0a6a874f9ea632a4487a366de9.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>不过，屏下指纹解锁的位置偏低，习惯一下握持的姿势就好。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/6f8ef0cf56144eb5a3da7e2490b84d58.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_6f8ef0cf56144eb5a3da7e2490b84d58.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>在处理器方面，它和上代荣耀50 SE一样，都是采用了联发科天玑900处理器，基于台积电6nm工艺制造，是入门5G芯片的中流砥柱。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/b2f687a6c7b04f36bd58da689f5cfb77.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_b2f687a6c7b04f36bd58da689f5cfb77.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>安兔兔实测423514分，超过了18%的用户，日常使用绝对够了。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/eba1f8d6b46a4c0a927b810d26a7ee89.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_eba1f8d6b46a4c0a927b810d26a7ee89.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>打一把《王者荣耀》来看看，最高画质下全程稳定60 FPS没有什么问题。《英雄联盟手游版》、《和平精英》也都可以流畅运行。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/f21769661f17465a89228c4ccddc9009.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_f21769661f17465a89228c4ccddc9009.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>接下来是影像能力，虽然没有一亿像素，但是荣耀60 SE依然搭载了一颗6400万像素高清主摄，直接来看样张。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/cd62b58266f94a4aadd9290342fd9397.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_cd62b58266f94a4aadd9290342fd9397.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>白天解析度优秀，夜晚能够清晰记录灯光和美景。要我说，这颗6400万像素的主摄，拍照效果跟一亿像素主摄相比毫不逊色。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/dc2cdf54db474c4a9ae592133dcba548.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_dc2cdf54db474c4a9ae592133dcba548.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/33e306106000486ba6e7840f9f2d5185.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_33e306106000486ba6e7840f9f2d5185.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/a51a39b2db524e738c9d071ded6b4f63.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_a51a39b2db524e738c9d071ded6b4f63.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/450f63a3e3204798b22a0b4410a95a92.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_450f63a3e3204798b22a0b4410a95a92.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>两颗副摄分别为广角和微距，前者像素为500万、后者为200万感光微距镜头，可以解锁更多的视角和玩法。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/9df1a2951d6748759aa698815cd1e90b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_9df1a2951d6748759aa698815cd1e90b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>荣耀60 SE依然保留了标志性的Vlog能力。在相机APP中，你可以找到各种拍摄模板做参考，从音乐到运镜，手把手制导，教你如何拍出有模有样的Vlog。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/520c7af5d1ad46eda148fc88c3f452e5.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_520c7af5d1ad46eda148fc88c3f452e5.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>此外，它还支持双景录像等拍摄功能，可以同时实现前摄后摄、后摄广角等镜头的搭配组合拍摄，拍视频也能一心二用。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/ea2252f077804014991c5fc7cd7e1c76.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_ea2252f077804014991c5fc7cd7e1c76.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>荣耀60 SE支持66W超级快充，采用的还是更高级的单电芯方案，效率高、机身薄、发热小，还更耐用。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/86da2b1cca9c49649e83a2e16b9c5449.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_86da2b1cca9c49649e83a2e16b9c5449.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>我把电量用到4%再开始充电，只用15分钟就充了55%左右，充满只需要40分钟。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/f9e8a36f59624d4d989a4cd0771e3fce.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_f9e8a36f59624d4d989a4cd0771e3fce.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>虽然它的电池容量仅有4300mAh，但实际续航并不差，用电池狗狗高强度测试，成绩达到了8个半小时，非常标准的续航表现，再搭配66W快充可以说不会有太大的电量焦虑。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/d7e988a902854bee9ebd30abe694909b.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_d7e988a902854bee9ebd30abe694909b.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>和上一代相比，荣耀60 SE虽然没有了越级的一亿像素主摄，但加入了素质更高的OLED曲面屏，整机质感也大幅提升。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/8736229fba754fd08919a0e4a715df7d.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_8736229fba754fd08919a0e4a715df7d.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p>最关键的当然是颜值更高了，正面曲面屏更具沉浸感，背部3D玻璃机身更加绚烂多彩，后摄模组也更具高级感，简直不像是一款“SE”级别的产品。</p>
<p align="center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/97bfb03b3a8f429db753d16fa3974fd8.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="2000元档高颜值双曲面屏手机！荣耀60 SE上手体验视频" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220210/s_97bfb03b3a8f429db753d16fa3974fd8.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid;" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
