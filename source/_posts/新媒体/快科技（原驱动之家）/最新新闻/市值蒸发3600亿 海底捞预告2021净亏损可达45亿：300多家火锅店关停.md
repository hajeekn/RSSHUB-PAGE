<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，海底捞发表公告称，预期将于截至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日止年度录得净亏损约人民币<span lang="EN-US">38</span>亿元至人民币<span lang="EN-US">45</span>亿元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">海底捞表示，对比<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年收入约为人民币<span lang="EN-US">286</span>亿元，<strong>本集团<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年收入预计超过人民币<span lang="EN-US">400</span>亿元，增长超过<span lang="EN-US">40%</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">预期亏损主要归因于：<span lang="EN-US">①2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">300</span>余家餐厅关停及餐厅经营业绩下滑等因素导致的处置长期资产的一次性损失、减值损失等合计约人民币<span lang="EN-US">33</span>亿元至人民币<span lang="EN-US">39</span>亿元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">②</span>全球持续变化和反复的疫情，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年、<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年门店网络快速扩张以及本公司内部管理问题等对海底捞餐厅经营状况带来的冲击。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，餐饮<span lang="EN-US">“</span>航母<span lang="EN-US">”</span>海底捞在港交所挂牌，发行价为<span lang="EN-US">17.8</span>港元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>股，盘中一度飙升至<span lang="EN-US">19.64</span>港元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>股，市值摸高<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>亿港元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，海底捞股价达到历史新高的<span lang="EN-US">85.75</span>港元<span lang="EN-US">/</span>股，总市值曾一度接近<span lang="EN-US">4700</span>亿港元，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>不过之后海底捞股价开始大幅下滑，目前股价只有<span lang="EN-US">20.2</span>港元，市值<span lang="EN-US">1126</span>亿港币，一年之中市值蒸发<span lang="EN-US">3600</span>亿港币。</strong></span></p>
