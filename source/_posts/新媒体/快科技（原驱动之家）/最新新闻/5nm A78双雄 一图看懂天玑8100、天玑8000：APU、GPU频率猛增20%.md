<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">今天下午，联发科发布了次旗舰芯片天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>及天玑<span lang="EN-US">8000</span>，<a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/817/817458.htm" target="_blank">它们都使用了台积电<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺及<span lang="EN-US">A78</span>架构，</a>差别主要是部分单元的频率上，其中天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>在<span lang="EN-US">APU</span>及<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>频率上要高出<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>，<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>及游戏性能更好。</p>
<p style="text-align: center"><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220301/c3d9654e-f798-4458-902f-dc7edd2d45c0.jpg" target="_blank"><img alt="5nm A78双雄 一图看懂天玑8100、天玑8000：APU、GPU频率猛增20%" h="337" src="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220301/Sc3d9654e-f798-4458-902f-dc7edd2d45c0.jpg" style="border: black 1px solid" w="600" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">根据官方公布的信息，天玑<span lang="EN-US">8000</span>的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>是<span lang="EN-US">4</span>核<span lang="EN-US">A78</span>大核<span lang="EN-US">+4</span>个<span lang="EN-US">A55</span>小核组成，<strong>前者最高频率<span lang="EN-US">2.75GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">A55</span>小核频率最高<span lang="EN-US">2.0GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">L3</span>缓存<span lang="EN-US">4MB</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>架构及小核频率都没变，<span lang="EN-US">L3</span>也是<span lang="EN-US">4MB</span>，只是<span lang="EN-US">A78</span>大核频率提升到<span lang="EN-US">2.85GHz</span>，增加了<span lang="EN-US">100MHz</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AI</span>单元中，<strong>天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>及天玑<span lang="EN-US">8000</span>都是<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个性能核<span lang="EN-US">+1</span>个通用核，差别是性能核的频率提升了<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>方面，天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>及天玑<span lang="EN-US">8000</span>都是<span lang="EN-US">ARM</span>的<span lang="EN-US">Mali-G610 MP6</span>，也就是<span lang="EN-US">6</span>核<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>但天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>的频率提升<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>，游戏性能更强。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">显示方面，天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>与天玑<span lang="EN-US">8000</span>都支持<span lang="EN-US">FHD+1 68Hz</span>高刷、<span lang="EN-US">HDR10+</span>自适应，不过天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>还多支持<span lang="EN-US">WQHD+120Hz</span>，更高端一些。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">内存方面，天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>及天玑<span lang="EN-US">8000</span>都是<span lang="EN-US">LPDDR5-6400Mbps</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">5G</span>及<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi</span>方面，天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>及天玑<span lang="EN-US">8000</span>没有区别，都是<span lang="EN-US">5G R16</span>、<span lang="EN-US">2CC</span>多载波、下线<span lang="EN-US">4.7Gbps</span>、<span lang="EN-US">R16</span>超级上行、<span lang="EN-US">Wi-Fi 6E</span>、蓝牙<span lang="EN-US">5.3</span>等等。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">影响方面，天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>及天玑<span lang="EN-US">8000</span>的<span lang="EN-US">ISP</span>单元也没有区分，都是<span lang="EN-US">3x14bit ISP</span>架构，性能可达每秒<span lang="EN-US">50</span>亿像素，支持<span lang="EN-US">2</span>摄像头并行拍摄，支持<span lang="EN-US">3200</span>万<span lang="EN-US">+3200</span>万<span lang="EN-US">+1600</span>像素三摄。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">总体来看，天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>及天玑<span lang="EN-US">8000</span>的差距很小，只要是<span lang="EN-US">AI</span>及<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>频率提升<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>，玩游戏的话自然是天玑<span lang="EN-US">8100</span>更强一些，其他方面体验差别不大。</p>
