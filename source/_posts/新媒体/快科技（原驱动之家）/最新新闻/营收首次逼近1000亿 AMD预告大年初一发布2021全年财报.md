<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年过去了，很快就到了欧美公司的财报季了，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>公司日前发布预告，将于<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日发布<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">Q4</span>季度及全年财报，那一天也是国内的大年初一。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年虽然有全球疫情的影响，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>但这也将是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>史上业绩最好的一年，官方此前预告全年营收增长<span lang="EN-US">60%</span>，不过<span lang="EN-US">Q3</span>季度中增速已经调高到<span lang="EN-US">65%</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>公司在<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年实现营收<span lang="EN-US">97.6</span>亿美元，经营收入<span lang="EN-US">13.7</span>亿美元，净收入<span lang="EN-US">24.9</span>亿美元，摊薄后每股收益<span lang="EN-US">2.06</span>美元——<strong>以此计算的话，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年的营收将达到<span lang="EN-US">160</span>亿美元，接近<span lang="EN-US">1000</span>亿人民币了。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>公布<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年第三季度财报，当季营业额为<span lang="EN-US">43</span>亿美元，同比大涨<span lang="EN-US">54%</span>，经营收入为<span lang="EN-US">9.48</span>亿美元，净收入为<span lang="EN-US">9.23</span>亿美元，摊薄后每股收益为<span lang="EN-US">0.75</span>美元。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其中，计算与图形事业部营业额为<span lang="EN-US">24</span>亿美元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">44%</span>，环比增长<span lang="EN-US">7%</span>，主要得益于锐龙处理器、<span lang="EN-US">Radeon</span>显卡和<span lang="EN-US"> AMD Instinct </span>加速器销量增长。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">企业、嵌入式和半定制事业部的营业额为<span lang="EN-US">19</span>亿美元，同比增长<span lang="EN-US">69%</span>，环比增长<span lang="EN-US">20%</span>，主要得益于<span lang="EN-US">EPYC</span>（霄龙）处理器和半定制产品销量的增加。</p>
