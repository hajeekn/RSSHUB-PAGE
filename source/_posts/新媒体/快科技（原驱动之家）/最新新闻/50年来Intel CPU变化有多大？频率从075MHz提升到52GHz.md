<p class="MsoNormal"><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/796/796355.htm" target="_blank">今天（11月15日）是Intel推出4004处理器50周年的日子</a>，当初为日本计算器客户开发的芯片成为全球首个微处理器，并且改变了<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>的发展，从当初的内存芯片转型为处理器，成就了<span lang="EN-US">50</span>年的霸业，意义非凡。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Intel</span><a class="f14_link" href="https://www.intel.com/content/www/us/en/newsroom/resources/intel-4004.html" target="_blank">官网</a>上也发表了<span lang="EN-US">4004</span>处理器<span lang="EN-US">50</span>周年的纪念文章，有不少史料可供查阅，这里不再赘述，来看看<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>做的一个有趣对比。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">50</span>年后<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>最新的<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>已经是<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿，<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月底发布，本月初才上市，<strong>那<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿跟<span lang="EN-US">4004</span>处理器有多达差别呢，可以看下面的长图对比。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">当年<span lang="EN-US">4004</span>处理器只有<span lang="EN-US">2300</span>个晶体管，如今的<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿晶体管数量是数十亿计（<span lang="EN-US">Intel</span>最近几代<span lang="EN-US">CPU</span>很少公布具体的晶体管数量了），这方面提升了数万乃至数十万。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">工艺就不用说了，<span lang="EN-US">4004</span>当年使用的还是<span lang="EN-US">2</span>英寸晶圆，<span lang="EN-US">10um</span>工艺，<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿是<span lang="EN-US">Intel 7</span>工艺、<span lang="EN-US">12</span>英寸晶圆。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">性能方面，<span lang="EN-US">4004</span>是单核单线程芯片，频率只有<span lang="EN-US">750KMHz</span>，也就不到<span lang="EN-US">1MHz</span>，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>而<span lang="EN-US">12</span>代酷睿做到了<span lang="EN-US">16</span>核<span lang="EN-US">24</span>线程，频率可达<span lang="EN-US">5.2GHz</span>，频率这一个指标就提升了将近<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>倍，</strong></span>算上核心数的提升，性能提升估计能上<span lang="EN-US">10</span>万倍，毕竟<span lang="EN-US">50</span>年的积累之后完全是碾压式的存在。</p>
