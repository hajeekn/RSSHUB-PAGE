<p class="MsoNormal">从<span lang="EN-US">7nm Zen2</span>处理器开始，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>就走上了小芯片设计之路，将多个小芯片封装在一起实现高性能，而显卡也会从下代的<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>开始走向多芯片封装。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">此前报道，<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构的旗舰是<span lang="EN-US">Navi 31</span>核心，命名上应该是<span lang="EN-US">RX 7900</span>系列，采用双芯片封装，分为两个所谓的<span lang="EN-US">GCD</span>模块<span lang="EN-US">(</span>概念等同锐龙处理器里的<span lang="EN-US">CCD)</span>，<span lang="EN-US">5nm</span>工艺制造，以及一个<span lang="EN-US">MCD</span>模块<span lang="EN-US">(</span>类似锐龙的<span lang="EN-US">IOD)</span>，<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>工艺制造。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">Navi 31</span>会集成多达<span lang="EN-US">15360</span>个流处理器<span lang="EN-US">(ALU</span>单元<span lang="EN-US">)</span>、<span lang="EN-US">512MB</span>无限缓存，分别是现在<span lang="EN-US">Navi 21</span>核心的三倍、四倍，搭配<span lang="EN-US">256-bit GDDR6</span>位宽，核心频率可达<span lang="EN-US">2.4GHz</span>到<span lang="EN-US">2.5GHz</span>，<span lang="EN-US">FP32</span>浮点性能可达<span lang="EN-US">75TFLOPS</span>，性能比<span lang="EN-US">RX 6900 XT</span>提升<span lang="EN-US">200%</span>以上。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">最新消息来看，<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构的双芯封装非常复杂，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>不是简单地两个芯片模块结合就完事，而是先进的<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>封装技术，其技术复杂度比现在的<span lang="EN-US">MI200</span>系列还要高，挑战很大。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">技术难度高通常也意味着成本高昂，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>明年底也要发布<span lang="EN-US">RDNA3</span>架构显卡的，<strong>估计<span lang="EN-US">RX 7000</span>系列也会涨价，跟<span lang="EN-US">RTX 40</span>系列一样，就看性能是否可以全面压制<span lang="EN-US">NVIDIA</span>了，</strong>毕竟上了这么复杂的<span lang="EN-US">3D</span>封装。</p>
