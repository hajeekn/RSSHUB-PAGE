<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>发布会还有不到一天时间，这次发布的新品重点是锐龙<span lang="EN-US">6000</span>系列处理器，不过显卡方面可能会有两款<span lang="EN-US">Navi24</span>核的主流卡——<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>及<span lang="EN-US">RX 6400</span>，它们会升级<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>工艺。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">目前的<span lang="EN-US">RDNA2</span>架构<span lang="EN-US">Navi2X</span>系列<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>核心是台积电<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>工艺的，不过<span lang="EN-US">6nm</span>跟<span lang="EN-US">7nm</span>设计上是兼容的，密度更高，性能提升不大，但能效上多少有点优势。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">从最新爆料来看，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>显卡的频率会是个亮点，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>有消息人士称游戏频率可达<span lang="EN-US">2610MHz</span>，加速频率最高可达<span lang="EN-US">2815MHz</span>，这可是官方显卡中的新高了，比目前水平至少高出<span lang="EN-US">10%</span>左右。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">显存频率这次也会有惊喜，虽然没有用上<span lang="EN-US">GDDR6X</span>，<strong>但<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>配备的是<span lang="EN-US">18Gbps GDDR6</span>显存，</strong>能效上比<span lang="EN-US">GDDR6X</span>更有优势。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">其他方面，<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>显卡的规格也曝光差不多了，<span lang="EN-US">1024</span>个流处理器，<span lang="EN-US">16</span>个光追单元，<span lang="EN-US">16MB</span>无限缓存，<span lang="EN-US">64-bit 4GB GDDR6</span>显存，带宽<span lang="EN-US">128GB/s</span>，功耗<span lang="EN-US">107W</span>，<span lang="EN-US">6</span>针辅助供电，单风扇散热，全高双插槽短卡，<span lang="EN-US">DisplayPort</span>、<span lang="EN-US">HDMI</span>输出接口。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">现在就等<span lang="EN-US">CES</span>发布会官宣了，<span lang="EN-US">4GB</span>显存虽然看上去比较小，<strong>但这样的设计会让矿卡用户减少对它的需求，更多地满足游戏玩家，本质上是好事。</strong></p>
