<div>   
<p class="MsoNormal">相比酷睿，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>的锐龙处理器桌面版一直没有集成核显，只有锐龙<span lang="EN-US">APU</span>才有核显，这是<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>处理器的一个优势，但某方面也是个缺点，今年的<span lang="EN-US">5nm Zen4</span>架构锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>上会改变，桌面版锐龙也要集成核显<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>了。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">不过对它的性能不要期待太多，德国<span lang="EN-US">computerbase</span>网站援引爆料玩家遠坂小町的消息称，<span style="color:#ff0000;"><strong>锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>的核显<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>只有<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个<span lang="EN-US">WGP</span>，也就是<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个<span lang="EN-US">CU</span>单元，频率约为<span lang="EN-US">1.1GHz</span>，算下来浮点性能约为<span lang="EN-US">0.5TFLOPS</span>。</strong></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这性能是什么水平呢？原文以<span lang="EN-US">steam deck</span>做了对比，后者是<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个<span lang="EN-US">RDNA2</span>架构的<span lang="EN-US">CU</span>单元，频率可达<span lang="EN-US">1.6GHz</span>，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>的核显性能约为后者<span lang="EN-US">1/3</span>。</p>
<p class="MsoNormal">这个可能还不好理解，<strong>如果以<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>最新的<span lang="EN-US">RX 6500 XT</span>显卡为例，其功耗为<span lang="EN-US">107W</span>，浮点性能约为<span lang="EN-US">5.8flops</span>，锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>的核显不到后者的<span lang="EN-US">1/10</span>。</strong></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">现在还无法确定锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>处理器的核显<span lang="EN-US">GPU</span>最终是否只有这一款，不过总体来看<span lang="EN-US">Zen4</span>这一代就算桌面版给了核显，其性能也很一般，<span lang="EN-US">AMD</span>做到亮机就行，毕竟锐龙<span lang="EN-US">7000</span>定位不低，玩家基本上都要搭配高性能独显的，没必要在核显上下功夫。</p>
