<p> 春节将至，团圆、喜庆、祥和、祝福，都是年味里最温馨的味道。然而，对于昆明出入境边检站的民警而言，在阖家团圆的日子里，他们绝大多数铆在岗位上，用忠诚与坚守，守护着新春佳节的祥和安宁。</p><p> 在三尺验证台，他们温暖着出入境旅客的回家路；在凌晨的停机坪，他们通宵达旦，全力保障货运航班安全顺畅；在执行“7+7+7”独立作战单元勤务模式中，执勤民警与家人的全家福，只能在视频里。年，越来越近，边检民警的年味是忠诚、担当、敬业、奉献……这样的滋味，让我们通过一组海报一起来品味。</p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/31/25348d29de5289a713e3da6ce337579d.jpeg" data-size="600,899" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/31/5b64cadf2dd5a436ee0fd3ff67d6d9a1.jpeg" data-size="600,899" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/31/51b2281feb6c87354ff2db91ab197ac7.jpeg" data-size="600,899" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/31/c1de4765527316678b80d89ade199e37.jpeg" data-size="600,899" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/31/e8481a93b5ef571d82be4df217670494.jpeg" data-size="600,899" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/31/58f852287d4a52add4d6f6cfd8b9ce96.jpeg" data-size="600,899" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/31/fdc59b08f85ad2ef61ecda9da7128d0e.jpeg" data-size="600,899" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/31/a56dce3ab5748a39fd35ce710ca2b9cf.jpeg" data-size="600,899" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/01/31/e73982bbeeddc611bf19eeab6f525bcf.jpeg" data-size="600,899" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"><p> 云南网通讯员 梁靖雯 王瑞东 摄影报道</p>
