<p> 近期天寒地冻，由于安全防范不当，火灾、车祸和窒息中毒等亡人事故时有发生。为了您和家人平安，应急管理部门紧急提醒您做到“五必须五严防”：</p><p> 01<strong> 电器取暖必须人离电断，严防小火亡人。</strong></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/22/8dffdb03a392716aaafd83114e7a3603.jpeg" data-size="420,272" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p> 02<strong> 烧煤烧炭取暖必须开窗通风，严防窒息中毒。</strong></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/22/5945f8d8df4290ea3f402475609d49aa.jpeg" data-size="413,277" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p> 03<strong> 使用燃气热水器严禁直排室内，必须确保通风换气，严防暗受伤害。 </strong></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/22/bc84ba6d7ebe5547258acf845194b57d.jpeg" data-size="400,309" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p> 04<strong> 熏烤腊肉必须确保安全距离，严防引发火灾。</strong></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/22/63ac371cddd98b434bcd9b5bfb91d5fa.jpeg" data-size="1080,720" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p> 05<strong> 上路行车必须关注天气，杜绝超载超速和酒驾醉驾疲劳驾，严防乐极生悲。</strong></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/22/6fce59b5b8173f1958c906767e1ad6fb.jpeg" data-size="500,375" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p> 责编：唐锦玲</p><p> 来源：冷水江市融媒体中心</p><p><span><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/22/3f2ce224fb18ec1949e056844d3a5504.jpeg" data-size="900,333" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></span></p><p>【来源：冷水江新闻网_社会】</p><p>版权归原作者所有，向原创致敬</p>
