<p>原标题：今晚起至大年初三白天湘中以北局地暴雪，这几件事驾驶人请注意</p><p>据省气象台预计，大年初一至大年初三省内有一次低温雨雪天气过程。其中初一晚至初三白天，湘中以北局地暴雪；初四早晨，湘西、湘中以北大部分地区有雾。</p><p>低温雨雪冰冻等恶劣天气来袭，湖南高速警察提醒您：非必要不驾车出行！春节期间走亲访友，确有出行计划的，应在出行前应留意天气、路况信息，尽量避开雨雪天气或受恶劣天气影响的路段。同时湖南交警同样会持续坚守岗位，为大家的平安出行保驾护航。</p><p>同时提醒各位驾驶人，在冰雪路面行车，以下安全知识请您一定要牢记。</p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/e4141750b11d8e417b8e3d6d49298546.jpeg" data-size="480,422" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/93bfbab9becbab0cbb6b88feb56269fc.jpeg" data-size="480,422" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/cbb828b335d54477fbdcb1ebc80f02b3.jpeg" data-size="480,422" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/01d11260f9bd71f7e77af6d18c02e6fa.jpeg" data-size="480,422" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/e0edd22824d913ecb92f936ecdf0c243.jpeg" data-size="480,422" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/f1130cce51a208c2e05084e67e210ab0.jpeg" data-size="480,422" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/f53c149b1cc086ab7ff7ba241d13607b.jpeg" data-size="480,422" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://cors.zfour.workers.dev/?http://static.1sapp.com/qupost/img/2022/02/02/221529bd32b7c6cbf39ed48ab5f0ce95.jpeg" data-size="480,422" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>（肖帅 伍波峰）</p><p>来源：红网</p>
