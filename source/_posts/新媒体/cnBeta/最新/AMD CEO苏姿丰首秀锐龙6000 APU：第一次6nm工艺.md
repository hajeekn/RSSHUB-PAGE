发布会还没开始，“苏妈”就忍不住了！<strong>AMD CEO苏姿丰博士在个人Twitter上，公开了锐龙6000 APU处理器的第一张实物照片。</strong><br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220104/68a8853e4011408eb97cb24ad255747d.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0104/684940121c2c32a.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0104/684940121c2c32a.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0104/684940121c2c32a.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220104/479b7067607947958735a8c713e0e9ed.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0104/0cbcef4ab4b022f.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0104/0cbcef4ab4b022f.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0104/0cbcef4ab4b022f.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a class="f14_link" href="https://news.mydrivers.com/1/806/806786.htm" target="_blank">其实之前我们就看过它的曝光照</a>，确切地说是移动端的锐龙6000H/6000U，代号Rembrandt，采用新的FP7封装方式，制造工艺则从7nm升级到6nm。</p><p>这也是<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://amd-cpu.jd.com/" target="_blank">AMD</a>的第一颗6nm处理器，此外RX 6000M系列移动端显卡、RX 6500 XT/RX 6400桌面入门显卡，也会都是6nm。</p><p>锐龙6000H/6000U系列据说会使用升级版的Zen3+ CPU架构，GPU则第一次升级到RDNA2，并支持PCIe 4.0。</p><p>苏姿丰还表示，今年会有很多新技术，大家敬请期待。</p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0104/4234054781b7c1b.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0104/223a22ef44624fa.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p>
