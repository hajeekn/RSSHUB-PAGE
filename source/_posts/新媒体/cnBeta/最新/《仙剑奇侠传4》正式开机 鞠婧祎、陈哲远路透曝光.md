<strong>今日（2月16日），据新浪娱乐报道，《仙剑奇侠传4》（后更名为《天河传》）正式开机，鞠婧祎、陈哲远路透图曝光。</strong>在剧中，鞠婧祎将饰演韩菱纱，陈哲远将饰演云天河。<br>
 <p style="text-align: left;">《天河传》是根据上海软星单机游戏《仙剑奇侠传4》改编的古装仙侠剧，由爱奇艺、上海新文化传媒集团股份有限公司和新媒诚品出品。导演朱锐斌，主演陈哲远 / 鞠婧祎。讲述性格率真的云天河，自幼失怙。一次偶然的情况下邂逅闯入父亲墓室的少女韩菱纱，两人从此结下不解之缘。</p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/02/16/origin_202202161225035967.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0216/1288a29800c3e81.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/02/16/origin_202202161225031260.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0216/dda483edafa2a78.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align:center"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/02/16/origin_202202161225047132.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0216/3a513ddcca2ea24.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p style="text-align: center;"><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/02/16/origin_202202161225045963.jpg"><img src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0216/b754946a2316eaf.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
