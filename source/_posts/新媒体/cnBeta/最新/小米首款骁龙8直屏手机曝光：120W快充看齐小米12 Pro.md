2月16日消息，小米POCO F4 GT获得了FCC认证，<strong>型号为21121210G，该机即将在海外登场。</strong>科技媒体GSMArena指出，考虑到上一代机型小米POCO F3 GT对应的是国行版机型Redmi K40游戏增强版，因此<strong>小米POCO F4 GT对应的机型应该是Redmi K50电竞版。</strong><br>
 <p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0216/cfd36003f869a0d.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0216/cfd36003f869a0d.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0216/cfd36003f869a0d.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>这意味着小米POCO F4 GT拥有和<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&aid=450&euid=&t=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.mi.com%2F" target="_blank">Redmi</a> K50电竞版相同的硬件配置。具体包括6.67英寸<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_88234%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D2_1_0%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">OLED</a>柔性直屏、骁龙8处理器、6400万主摄、4700mAh电池和120W神仙秒充等。</p><p>从配置来看，<strong>小米POCO F4 GT是小米系列第一款采用直屏方案的骁龙8旗舰<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>，</strong>此前上市的小米12、小米12 Pro等骁龙8机型都是<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https%3A%2F%2Flist.jd.com%2Flist.html%3Fcat%3D737%2C794%2C798%26ev%3D4155_92263%26sort%3Dsort_rank_asc%26trans%3D1%26JL%3D3_%25E7%2594%25B5%25E8%25A7%2586%25E7%25B1%25BB%25E5%259E%258B_%25E6%259B%25B2%25E9%259D%25A2%23J_crumbsBar" target="_blank">曲面</a>屏方案。</p><p>另外，<strong>小米POCO F4 GT的120W秒充也是比肩小米12 Pro</strong>，后者同样支持120W秒充。区别在于小米POCO F4 GT为双电芯方案，而小米12 Pro为单电芯方案。</p><p>预计小米不久就会在海外官宣这款新品。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0216/f1532a53f2ddeaf.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0216/f1532a53f2ddeaf.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0216/f1532a53f2ddeaf.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
