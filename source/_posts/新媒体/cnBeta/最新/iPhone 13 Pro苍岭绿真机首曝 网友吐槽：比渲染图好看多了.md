今天凌晨的春季发布会上，苹果公布了iPhone 13 Pro全新的“苍岭绿”配色，并宣布将与3月18日正式开售。目前，爆料人AppleDsign已经提前拿到了这款新配色的iPhone 13 Pro，<strong>首次对外展示了这款手机的实拍照片。</strong><br>
 <p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220309/e99a4f7ea51d46f1a686f2790420fbd1.jpg" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0309/36f076286691abd.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0309/36f076286691abd.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0309/36f076286691abd.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220309/06ebbddb158e4a75bbef9a9f6d4689b1.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0309/7c1ac3213f7089a.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0309/7c1ac3213f7089a.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0309/7c1ac3213f7089a.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>对比后不难看出，<strong>苍岭绿色的真机有着比渲染图更低的饱和度</strong>，这不仅使得<a data-link="1" href="https://c.duomai.com/track.php?site_id=242986&euid=&t=https://shouji.jd.com/" target="_blank">手机</a>看起来更亮，也使得其观感更加淡雅，而不是像渲染图所展示的那么“强烈”且具有视觉冲击性。</p><p>同时，苍岭绿配色所采用的哑光玻璃背板，有效提升了后壳的整体质感，也一并解决了玻璃后壳沾指纹等老大难问题。</p><p>而在网友看来，这次的苍岭绿配色，<strong>无疑成为了被渲染图“坑害”的典型案例。</strong></p><p>有网友表示，看渲染图觉得新配色是头盔绿，结果看了真机照片才知道实际上是抹茶绿；也有网友直言，这真机图比渲染图好看多了。</p><p>目前，苍岭绿配色的<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fiphone%2F" target="_blank">iPhone</a> 13 Pro已经在<a data-link="1" href="https://apple.pvxt.net/c/1251234/435400/7639?u=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.apple.com%2Fcn%2Fmusic%2F" target="_blank">苹果</a>官方商城上架，售价7999元起，<strong>但仍需等到3月11日后才能够下单购买。</strong></p><p><a href="https://img1.mydrivers.com/img/20220309/9c439520911c44509314ccc57e51f39c.png" target="_blank"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0309/859e2078f9f05f7.png"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0309/859e2078f9f05f7.png" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0309/859e2078f9f05f7.png" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
