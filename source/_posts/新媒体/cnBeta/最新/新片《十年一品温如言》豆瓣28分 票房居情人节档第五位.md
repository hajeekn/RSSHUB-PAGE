根据书海沧生同名原著小说改编，由赵非执导，丁禹兮、任敏、李泽锋等主演的国产爱情片《十年一品温如言》豆瓣开局分数出炉，截至发稿前本片为2.8分，有超过1万人进行评分，以及6600条短评。<br>
 <p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/02/15/origin_202202151224375504.jpg"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0215/45ae0a6e1a0d335.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0215/45ae0a6e1a0d335.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0215/45ae0a6e1a0d335.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>根据网友评价来看，本片的改编几乎完全脱离原著小说，有人认为影评故事情节与节奏相当令人无语，选在情人节上映，割书粉和节日情侣双重韭菜。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/02/15/origin_202202151139187003.jpg"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0215/fc2130488185098.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0215/fc2130488185098.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0215/fc2130488185098.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p><p>虽然电影的评价堪忧，但得益于在2022情人节上映，影片昨日票房成绩达到1.45亿元，单日成绩甚至超过了《长津湖之水门桥》。不过在短暂的高光过后，今日本片票房成绩已下滑至大盘第五位，位列《熊出没·重返地球》之后。</p><p><a target="_blank" href="https://www.gamersky.com/showimage/id_gamersky.shtml?https://img1.gamersky.com/upimg/pic/2022/02/15/origin_202202151139185679.jpg"></a><a target="_blank" href="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0215/91890e27ca083be.jpg"><img data-original="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/article/2022/0215/91890e27ca083be.jpg" src="https://static.cnbetacdn.com/thumb/article/2022/0215/91890e27ca083be.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a></p>
