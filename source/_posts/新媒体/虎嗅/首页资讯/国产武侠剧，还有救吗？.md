referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>注意，<strong>这已经是开了二倍速之后的效果。</strong></p><p>落地后终于等到主角出招。可镜头却是脸部特写。</p><p>到底要让观众看脸还是看身手？</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074811998495.gif" data-w="545" data-h="276" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074811998495.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>漫长的颜值展示过后。只见她反手就是一巴掌，打出了漫画效果。</p><p>所有对手高高飞起，再狠狠落地。</p><p>结束了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074814523809.gif" data-w="545" data-h="276" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074814523809.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>说实话，看完这段打戏，鱼叔整个人是懵的。</p><p>除了ending pose，根本不知道中间发生了什么。甚至连一个完整的打斗回合都没拍出来。</p><p><strong>这是武侠还是玄幻？</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074816798833.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="750" data-h="209" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074816798833.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074816831725.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="750" data-h="296" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074816831725.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这是个例吗？</p><p>当然不是。</p><p>纵观近几年国产武侠剧中的打戏，总让人感觉：</p><p><strong>如今的国产武侠剧，一个能打的都没有。</strong></p><p>当该剧打戏被吐槽时。</p><p>评论区有网友借此安利隔壁《风起洛阳》的打戏。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074817289883.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="972" data-h="195" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074817289883.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>可却被人扒出疑似抄袭动画《凡人修仙传》。</p><p>有UP主将两部剧的打戏同屏对比。发现不只是动作一样，就连分镜头也一样。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074818588323.gif" data-w="543" data-h="300" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074818588323.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074820364200.gif" data-w="543" data-h="300" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074820364200.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">视频来源：B站@伤影灬<br></p><p>《凡人修仙传》的动作导演亲自下场。</p><p>放出一段真人动作捕捉的花絮回应此事。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074824810682.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="854" data-h="761" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074824810682.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>接着又有越来越多雷同的镜头被扒出来。</p><p>完全是对照着拍出来的。</p><p>分镜头中的人物站位，包括节奏与停顿，慢动作的时间点，也完全一致。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074826053035.gif" data-w="350" data-h="390" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074826053035.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074827925141.gif" data-w="350" data-h="390" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074827925141.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>果然，《风起洛阳》官方后来公开道歉，并且承认抄袭。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074828797390.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="960" data-h="1026" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074828797390.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这时看见有网友评论说：</p><p>“国产剧已经沦落到需要去抄动画了吗？”</p><p>此言差矣。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074829678929.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="314" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074829678929.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在鱼叔看来，<strong>近几年真正做到传承武侠文化的，恰恰就是动画。</strong></p><p>除了上文提到的《凡人修仙传》。还有水墨画风的《雾山五行》。用狂野的粗线条，来呈现武打动作，让画面看上去更具表现力。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074832724185.gif" data-w="379" data-h="179" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074832724185.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074835333765.gif" data-w="379" data-h="179" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074835333765.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>火出国门的《刺客伍六七》也是从头打到尾。各种充满想象力的打戏，剪辑流畅，动作华丽。又不失黑色幽默的风格。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074837220977.gif" data-w="569" data-h="311" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074837220977.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074839415279.gif" data-w="474" data-h="259" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074839415279.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>还有《一人之下》中一对三的名场面。</p><p>一招一式，有板有眼。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074841045662.gif" data-w="543" data-h="274" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074841045662.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这些动画中的打戏，无论是动作还是分镜设计，都足以吊打如今所有的国产武侠剧。</p><p>反观近些年国产武侠剧中的打戏。除了抄袭动画片，大部分都很拉胯。</p><p>今年另一部武侠剧《有翡》，剧中的打戏同样令人出戏。</p><p>看上去和开头提到的是同一套模式：</p><p><strong>先摆造型，再转圈，瞎挥一通，对手全部应声倒下。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074843261499.gif" data-w="529" data-h="233" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074843261499.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074846289083.gif" data-w="529" data-h="233" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074846289083.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>动作设计得过于简单，看上去也毫无力量感。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074849199413.gif" data-w="529" data-h="233" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074849199413.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>另外就是武侠剧打戏的一个通病：</p><p><strong>很多看起来无关紧要的动作，愣是要重放几次。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074851295676.gif" data-w="529" data-h="234" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074851295676.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>还有两部翻拍的金庸剧。</p><p>2019年的《新倚天屠龙记》，打戏也是大量慢动作。</p><p>招数笨拙，表情轻浮，实难与“侠”字相挂钩。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074853151158.gif" data-w="366" data-h="192" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074853151158.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>今年的《新天龙八部》，选角和剧情魔改的雷点就暂且不说了。</p><p>看看这粗糙糊弄的打戏，搓麻将一般的降龙十八掌，对得起“金庸剧”的头衔吗？</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074855484302.gif" data-w="480" data-h="270" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074855484302.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>要说近几年，国产剧中唯一令人印象深刻的打戏。或许也就剩2018年《将夜》中的“春风亭雨夜大战”。</p><p>敌我双方缓缓靠近，渲染肃杀气氛，直至利剑出鞘。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074858378616.gif" data-w="454" data-h="219" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074858378616.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>有快有慢，动静结合。</p><p>镜头的剪辑也不会快到观众看不清招数动作。</p><p>战至中断，大雨倾盆而下，电闪雷鸣间进一步烘托悲壮情绪。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074859284610.gif" data-w="454" data-h="219" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074859284610.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074903920070.gif" data-w="454" data-h="219" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074903920070.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>吐槽完如今的武侠剧，我们不妨再回看曾经的武侠剧。</p><p>很多古早的武侠剧，囿于当时的条件，尽管特效五毛，布景廉价。但真正的核心部分——打戏，绝对是一点也不掉链子。</p><p>每个动作都交代得干净利落。几乎都是硬桥硬马，拳拳到肉。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074907044759.gif" data-w="339" data-h="235" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074907044759.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">《天下第一》<br></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074909512665.gif" data-w="411" data-h="261" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074909512665.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">《水月洞天》<br></p><p>包括中招后的反应，也经过精心设计，避免重复。而且对打戏越是自信，拍法往往也越质朴。</p><p>只需要镜头缓慢跟随主体的运动，往往都是一镜到底。观众看着有代入感，也足够过瘾。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074910975168.gif" data-w="344" data-h="220" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074910975168.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">《太极宗师》</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074912209295.gif" data-w="393" data-h="253" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074912209295.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">《侠客行》<br></p><p>这些还只是基本操作。更高阶的打戏，不只注重动作的连贯。更追求一种写意的美感。</p><p>这不仅需要武术指导的调教，也需要演员的刻苦训练。</p><p>最具代表性的是《神雕侠侣》刘亦菲饰演的小龙女。</p><p>一袭白衣，踏月而来，仅出场动作便至今难忘。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074915081623.gif" data-w="577" data-h="316" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074915081623.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074924230702.gif" data-w="494" data-h="270" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074924230702.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>小龙女的武器是白绸带，所有较少近身肉搏。</p><p>但是每次挥舞都要注重施力，确保灵动飘逸。</p><p>剧中的打戏，无不是美与力的结合，飘逸潇洒。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074927464623.gif" data-w="494" data-h="270" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074927464623.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074929107907.gif" data-w="450" data-h="229" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074929107907.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>得益于刘亦菲扎实的基本功。</p><p>底盘稳，核心力量强，能够演绎好小龙女一角。</p><p>就说其中上绳睡觉的这一片段，一字马四平八稳，足见功力。</p><p>对比后来陈妍希的版本，简直是公开处刑。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074931669198.gif" data-w="542" data-h="255" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074931669198.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074933067127.gif" data-w="542" data-h="255" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074933067127.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>如何让打戏再上升一个档次？</p><p>那就是在满足连贯与美感的同时，打戏还能进一步参与叙事，负责塑造和丰满人物的性格。</p><p>虽说用武侠剧与武戏电影作比较属于降维打击，但鱼叔还是想举这几个例子。</p><p>《一代宗师》中叶问与宫二在金楼客厅交手。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074936694904.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="478" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074936694904.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074938161665.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="57" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074938161665.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074939283171.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="481" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074939283171.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074941783266.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="56" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074941783266.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>六十四手对咏春，两人点到为止，充满气韵与风情。</p><p>“叶底藏花”藏的亦是宫二对叶问的情愫。亦如二人的关系，再如何缠斗，终有停手止戈的一刻。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074943562273.gif" data-w="533" data-h="230" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074943562273.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>从始至终，未砸烂室内任何一物。</p><p>纤毫之争，这才是打戏中慢动作的教学级用法。</p><p>而不是为了慢而慢，结果拍成了《疯狂动物城》中的树懒。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074944181063.gif" data-w="533" data-h="230" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074944181063.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074948773806.gif" data-w="533" data-h="230" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074948773806.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>再如《卧虎藏龙》中俞秀莲与玉娇龙的交手。</p><p>俞秀莲见玉娇龙使的是青冥宝剑，便从一开始就受制于此剑。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074950471540.gif" data-w="533" data-h="227" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074950471540.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>俞秀莲识剑识人。</p><p>出于对李慕白的深情，便只想夺回青冥宝剑，不忍伤害玉娇龙。</p><p>所以在交手中才处处留情，有所保留。</p><p>结果换了各种兵器，都毁于青冥宝剑。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074953093901.gif" data-w="533" data-h="227" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074953093901.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074955713381.gif" data-w="444" data-h="189" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074955713381.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>直到用断剑制服了玉娇龙。</p><p>这种恻隐之心，正衬俞秀莲内柔外刚的性格。</p><p>也正是如此，才让玉娇龙能够脱身。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074958863533.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="414" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/074958863533.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>通过对比武侠剧这十几年来的打戏变化，我们不难发现：</p><p>打戏，原本应该是一部武侠剧的最大看点。</p><p>因为十几年前的武侠剧。它们大多是根据金庸古龙的小说改编而来。</p><p>观众对于故事情节早就烂熟于心。</p><p>之所以愿意一遍又一遍地观看，就是冲着打戏来的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075001719835.gif" data-w="444" data-h="219" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075001719835.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">《天龙八部》<br></p><p>可如今，武侠剧中的打戏却成了最大槽点。</p><p>发生如此巨大的转变，究竟是为什么？</p><p>武侠剧的没落和倒退，归根结底可以总结为两个字：<strong>态度。</strong></p><p><strong>主创对于武侠剧的态度变了。</strong>演员们不会再为了一部戏而专门苦练武打动作。武戏靠武替代劳，该露脸的时候演员才亲自上场。所以才会经常能看到，在打戏中演员们的脸部丝毫看不出在发力。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075004080828.gif" data-w="539" data-h="270" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075004080828.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>还喜欢滥用慢动作和特效。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075007346211.gif" data-w="545" data-h="276" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075007346211.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>若是这种态度，就算请来再好的武术指导也无济于事。</p><p>比如上文提到的《有翡》。这部剧的武指也是《怪侠一枝梅》的武指。</p><p>没有对比就没有伤害。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075010018926.gif" data-w="388" data-h="198" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075010018926.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>尤其是饰演燕十三的刘诗诗，她本身有舞蹈功底。</p><p>在剧中同时表现出了轻盈感和力量感。</p><p>开拍前，她要和其他演员进入唐人影视的小白楼，统一训练打戏。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075012664781.gif" data-w="539" data-h="270" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075012664781.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>挽剑花，一字马，耍双刀，这些都只能算作基本功。</p><p>在今天，如果是其他女星能勉强练好其中一项，估计都值得买上热搜了。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075014689385.gif" data-w="450" data-h="250" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075014689385.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>其实，武指在武侠剧中的地位日渐式微，早就不是什么秘密了。</p><p>在这部专门讲述港片武指的纪录片《龙虎武师》中。</p><p>北上到内地成立工作室的元德导演就直言：“原来他们不认武术指导，只认导演。”</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075016630054.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075016630054.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075019145346.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="114" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075019145346.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075020710799.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="118" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075020710799.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075021774608.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="114" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075021774608.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075022439911.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="116" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075022439911.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>他不得以才从武指转行导演，其实心里是拒绝的。</p><p>因为“术业有专攻”，文戏他并不在行，他只想主抓武戏。</p><p>影视市场不重视武指的后果，必然是忽视打戏。所以拍出来的打戏被群嘲，那就是活该。</p><p>上个世纪，来自中国香港地区的这些“龙虎武师”，为了完成一个动作，真的可以豁出命去。</p><p>很多时候拍完一场戏，最先喊的不是“收工”，而是“救人”。</p><p>这股“龙虎武师”的拼劲在港片没落后，曾短暂在内地武侠剧中扎过根。</p><p>如今也不复存在。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075023786944.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075023786944.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075027479501.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="97" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075027479501.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>除了主创的态度，市场对待武侠剧的态度也变了。</strong></p><p>过去十几年间，武侠剧的格局，从个人恩怨的刀光剑影，升华到为国为民的侠之大者。</p><p>如今又倒退回小情小爱。因为武侠剧的受众远不如偶像剧来得多。</p><p>所以<strong>有很多剧只是披着“武侠”的外衣，内核还是偶像剧。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075029928796.gif" data-w="539" data-h="270" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075029928796.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这些被误读的武侠剧，又怎会认真对待打戏呢？</p><p>特效先行，慢镜凑数，也少了侠骨仁义，多了戏谑玩乐。</p><p>呼唤了这么多年的“武侠回潮”，最终只落了个<strong>“武侠群嘲”</strong>。</p><p>再加上武侠剧的成本更高，对演员和技术的要求也更严苛。面对如今的市场需求和观众审美。再去精心打造一部精品武侠剧，看上去像是一件吃力不讨好的事。</p><p>如同《大刺客》中王羽的那句经典台词：</p><p><strong>“这一把剑，这一腔热血，不知该交付给谁。”</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075032356378.gif" data-w="719" data-h="342" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075032356378.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>优秀的武侠剧或武侠电影，包含着中华武术文化。</p><p>这类影视剧，就如同动漫之于日本，超英电影之于美国。</p><p>本应该成为我们文化输出最有优势的主力军。就像当年《卧虎藏龙》拿下奥斯卡最佳外语片在内的四项大奖；《英雄》获得奥斯卡最佳外语片提名；《功夫》获得金球奖最佳外语片提名；《一代宗师》获得奥斯卡最佳摄影和最佳服装设计两项提名。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075035117367.gif" data-w="539" data-h="293" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/22/075035117367.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>可回过头看，当年我们以为武侠文化的输出才刚开始，未曾想那时竟已是尾声。</p><p>属于侠客的江湖逐渐远去，刀光剑影也变得黯淡。</p><p>徒留一声叹息：</p><p>我们的武侠剧，还能活过来吗？</p><p><span class="text-remarks" label="备注">本文来自微信公众号：</span><a
