<p><span class="text-remarks" label="备注">本文来自微信公众号：</span><a href="https://mp.weixin.qq.com/s/nxxUUCc66Qdn3HqcETca3g" target="_blank" rel="nofollow" style="text-decoration: none;"><span class="text-remarks">地球人研究报告（ID：diqiuren005）</span></a><span class="text-remarks">，作者：你的外星小姨，题图来自：网易云音乐</span></p><p>每当各大音乐APP出现“年度报告”的时候，每个人的朋友圈都会成了听歌人的集体团建现场。</p><p>人们纷纷祭出自己五光十色的歌单和最爱歌手，试图在社交圈内寻找灵魂共鸣。</p><p>每当这个时刻，人们对此的看法也会自动分为几类：</p><p>分享欲强的人，基本都表现得对此十分热衷，九宫格基本上都不够用。</p><p>与之相对，<strong>另一部分人的态度则是“根本不care”：</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153534310124.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="926" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153534310124.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>当然，这两拨人没有孰高孰低，只不过有着不同的社交偏好。</p><p>更值得我们注意的是，还有一小批人，明明平时分享欲爆棚，此刻却在这关键时刻没了声音……</p><p>这是因为，他们的“年度报告”里多少有些难言之隐。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153537224684.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="440" data-h="873" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153537224684.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">1</h3><p>人类的品味是流动的，但对音乐的热爱却永恒不变。音乐软件的年度报告，在每年年末最受大家伙的欢迎。</p><p>它不仅能让人们宣告属于自己的音乐品味，也能在忙碌的生活中为你与朋友创造来之不易的共同话题。</p><p>以今日在朋友圈刷屏的极大主流音乐APP的年度报告为例。不少在社交圈内常年潜水的人，都突然蹦出来宣告了自己的音乐属性。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153539935497.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="864" data-h="1920" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153539935497.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">老二次元了<br></p><p>这类年度报告，不仅能提醒你“听歌最晚的时间”，还能想起“某天重复听过N次的歌”等等……而对于爱回忆的人来说，音乐才是唤起往事的最好工具。</p><p>但这些APP也忽略了一件事：<strong>大部份人听音乐，其实都有心血来潮的时候。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153542573791.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1335" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153542573791.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而这些心血来潮的时刻，可能人们都不是特别想回忆起来……</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153545149449.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1007" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153545149449.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>听歌软件并不能分清你是在军训练歌还是享受音乐。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153546170854.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1317" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153546170854.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>它只会无情地记录下你每个反复听歌的时刻，并在年末残忍呈现。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153547213308.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1103" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153547213308.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>爱在深夜听相声的人在这种分享活动里格外吃亏。别人在深夜听伤心情歌歌颂自己寂寞的灵魂，而相声人却没这种机会：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153548210405.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="440" data-h="304" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153548210405.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>对于有更独特品味的人来说，音乐软件的年末总结也是一年一度的社死时刻。</p><p>因为并不是所有人都能欣赏“小寡妇”的韵律。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153549774214.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="565" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153549774214.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>与之相比，在听歌软件里练习听力的学生们似乎要更好一些。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153551796681.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1800" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153551796681.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>每年也都有心酸学生的年度歌手是“XX听力”。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153552866938.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="709" data-h="1344" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153552866938.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>当然，音乐品味没有高雅或低俗之分。只要你爱听，就是好歌。</p><p>无论你是否心底有黑暗的秘密：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153553461955.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1641" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153553461955.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>还是一心向善：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153554148960.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="767" data-h="1513" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153554148960.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>那就都有分享的自由和权利。</p><p>一些比较倒霉的粉丝除外。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153555821244.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="427" data-h="441" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153555821244.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">2</h3><p>近几年，不仅是听歌软件，各式各样的生活常用APP都开始搞起了类似的“年终总结”。</p><p>有些网友们都喜闻乐见，而有些则让人避之不及。</p><p>例如一部分社交软件，就忽略了当代人对社交私密性的需求：在这几年的年末，微博上总会有“微博年度报告能看访客”的流言出现。搞得各位人人自查，开始发表一连串“无意冒犯”宣言。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153557704795.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1092" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153557704795.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>购物软件也是同一个道理。在支付宝最开始搞“年度账单”的时候，就有人因此被弄的家破人亡。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153559996863.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1261" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153559996863.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">主要还是本人问题<br></p><p>除此之外，这些“年终报告”也有一个通病：<strong>它们只有一套文案模版，且并不适用于所有情景。</strong></p><p>以近年被喷的最惨的“淘宝回忆杀”为例。在这个总结用户淘宝生涯的报告中，文案十分精致且略带伤感，就是数据匹配起来不太准确。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153604724670.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1515" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153604724670.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在淘宝，只要你买活体动物就会被定性为“购买宠物”。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153608991570.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1503" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153608991570.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>哪怕初衷与之相反。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153612322004.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="809" data-h="582" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153612322004.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>只要你买能穿身上的就会被评为“为ta打扮”。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153615225876.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1431" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153615225876.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>没人懂淘宝是怎么理解“爱好”这回事的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153619764901.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="394" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153619764901.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>有时候甚至会感觉它在嘲讽。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153621033032.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="568" data-h="358" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153621033032.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>视频网站的年度报告也有类似问题。在几年前，一位倒霉蛋因为看视频时不慎睡着，就被B站的年度报告记下了浓墨重彩的一笔。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153623927994.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="800" data-h="549" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153623927994.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>视频网站的确很难去辨别用户当时神志是否清醒，它们只知道你在一遍遍循环。</p><p>不过从这些用户们的年度视频记录中，我们倒也的确能看出一些问题。比如一部分人脱发的原因：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153626273344.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="437" data-h="701" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153626273344.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>和他们“离猝死究竟有多近”：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153626978057.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1368" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153626978057.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">3</h3><p>现如今，“年度报告”已经成了互联网冲浪选手们年末最期待的活动。它能事无巨细地提醒你自己在这一年里都干了啥，比记日记还管用。</p><p>尽管有些时候，年度报告配上文案显得有些生拉硬拽略显矫情。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153633571121.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="865" data-h="912" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153633571121.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但大多数时候都会直戳人心窝子。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153636364461.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="431" data-h="788" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153636364461.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>虽说不爱分享这些年终报告的人，可能多少都有那么一点难言之隐。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153637171920.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="440" data-h="345" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153637171920.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但更多的可能是，大伙在年末要面对的各平台“总结”实在太多了，在最初的新鲜感过去后，实在是总结不动了。毕竟，<strong>用自己的个人数据帮平台打广告</strong>，转多了总有些不对劲……</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg"
