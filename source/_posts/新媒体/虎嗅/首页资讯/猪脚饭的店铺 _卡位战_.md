class="text-img-note">进入城中村的小巷子<br></p><p>巷子接近200米长，两边都是餐饮店，没细数大概40家，竞争非常激烈，基本上3-6个月就会迎来一波洗牌<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（倒闭换新店）</span>。</p><p>这天路过发现一件不寻常的事，巷子进去右侧第二间新开的店生意极其火爆，排队吃饭的人都挤到巷中间。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" class="text-img-note">左边的店，这是从巷里往外照<br></p><p>店里有6张桌，因客人太多，老板又在外边多放了一张折叠桌，那天晚上气温15°左右，食客竟愿意坐在冻风中吃饭。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153506006508.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="749" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153506006508.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>只是一间普通猪脚饭店，并不是什么神仙美食，况且巷子原本就有3间猪脚饭，生意一直不旺，这间店到底施了什么魔法？我决定以一个食客的角度揭开面纱。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">用陈列来 “卡位”</h3><p>我发现进入巷子的人大都会侧头看这间店，除了被客流吸引，眼光大多停留在食物上。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153507461421.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="749" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153507461421.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>老板非常聪明，他把肉菜铺开一大桌子，正对着进入巷子的客流，而且样式非常丰富，猪脚、煎鱼、烧鸭，还有各式炒菜，任何经过的饥肠辘辘的人都不会置之不理。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153508029839.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="749" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153508029839.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153509854951.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="721" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153509854951.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>发现一个细节，橱窗非常亮，老板在橱窗顶上装了3颗射灯和2条红光灯，超强的亮度打在油腻腻的食物上，视觉冲击非常强烈。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153510578742.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153510578742.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">店里拍的，看右侧橱窗的灯<br></p><p>对比下，这是隔壁快餐店的橱窗，只装了一条红光灯，昏暗暗无法激起人的食欲。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153512163284.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153512163284.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>上面昏暗的店叫 “食味仙”，猪脚饭店没开前，他几乎是巷里唯一一间提供各式炒菜的店，生意一直很好，新开的猪脚饭店 “卡位” 后，生意下降很厉害。</p><p>这天晚上6点半，我数了下猪脚饭店共有17个客人在堂食，隔壁食味仙只有3个。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153513828781.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="749" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153513828781.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">食味仙，右边2人是老板和老板娘<br></p><p>索性把巷子前面一段的餐饮店的在店人数都数了下，时间：周四晚6点半，红色数字为在店食客人数。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153515077796.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="705" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153515077796.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>新开猪脚饭店有种虹吸效应，由于位置靠前，后面几间同样提供饭食的在店人数都很少，但更后面的三湘名串则不受影响，估计因为品类不一样。</p><p>7点和7点50分我又分别数了在店客人数<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（红色数字，逗号分隔）</span>：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153516665008.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="675" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153516665008.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>发现越晚那些麻辣烫店的客流越猛，而新开猪脚饭店显得有些力不从心，原因是橱窗里的菜越来越少，吸引力直线下降，麻辣烫的都是冻品，补货迅速。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153518788014.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153518788014.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">砂锅麻辣烫</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153519955121.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153519955121.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">三湘名串<br></p><p>同时我还走到巷子中间看另外3间猪脚饭店，就1、2个食客，所以不是品类的原因导致客流多。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153520135404.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153520135404.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153520130482.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153520130482.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">巷子中间的2间猪脚饭，客流很少<br></p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">专业和细节</h3><p>我是第一次看到猪脚饭店有提供四点筋饭，四点筋就是猪脚上包裹着骨头的软筋，口感咸香Q弹，那天晚上我在这间新开的猪脚饭店点了一份，20元。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153522789073.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="749" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153522789073.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153523980030.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="372" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153523980030.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">菜单<br></p><p>除了猪脚饭，还提供潮汕特色的大肠饭、卤鹅饭，还有每次回家才能吃到的麻叶、潮汕鱼饭和红米肉，在广州这么多年，首次看到一间猪脚饭店能提供这么多潮汕特色美食，不由打心底称赞一声 “专业”。</p><p>饭和例汤免费且无限续，墙上写着粥也免费。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153524070720.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153524070720.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">干净卫生<br></p><p>为了在店里坐久一点，我续了3碗汤，发现锅里煮着劈成两半的一大只鸡，禁不住又续一碗。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153525550545.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1332" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153525550545.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">可惜碗不够大，不然半只鸡我也勺进碗啃肉<br></p><p>店里4人，老板、老板娘加2厨工，老板负责配菜，老板娘记单，两小工一个后厨一个前台擦桌子。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153526016862.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="749" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153526016862.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">拿着蓝色牌子，瘦瘦小小的就是老板娘，热情、嗓门大<br></p><p>老板老家在揭阳市下属的惠来县，90后，客人实在太多没问到其他信息。</p><p>晚上6点-6点半我数了一下订单数，半小时35份，听微信收款的报价声，客单价大概18元。</p><p>中途还看到老板娘用手机远程带娃，“8点前完成所有作业，回去就给你买巧克力”。问老板娘这边开到几点，说早上10点开到晚上10点。</p><p>吃完猪脚饭回去后胃没有痛，说明卫生还行，话说之前去另一城中村吃哈尔滨卷饼，吃完10分钟胃立刻抽痛，回去吞了颗斯达舒胶囊才好点。</p><p>第二天中午我又来，这次点了鱼饭套餐，18元，鱼肉质紧实，鱼味浓，比较新鲜，挑毛病就是麻叶炒得咸了点。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153528548962.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153528548962.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>本以为城中村中午没啥客流，没想那些老人妇女都跑来这吃。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153529688000.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="750" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153529688000.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">对面烧腊店开了，但店里一个客人都没，老板不时往这边投来憎恨的眼神<br></p><p>周五中午11点18分开始半个小时，一共40个订单，居然比晚上还多。</p><p>这条巷有好几间店铺贴着旺铺招租，比如红框里的五谷鱼粉和聚洲牛杂。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153530477921.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="673" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202112/28/153530477921.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>跑去找五谷鱼粉的老板聊了下，他的店租是9000元/月，6万8千转让费包所有电器，店实用面积30平，不管位置和面积都跟新开的猪脚饭很像，我拿它来推算猪脚饭店的利润。</p><p>简单点有客人的时间算中午11点-1点 2小时+晚上5点半-8点半 3小时，一共5小时，每小时订单数假设70份，客单价18元，则一个月营业额是 18*70*5*30天=18.9万。</p><p>毛利率算60%，店租成本9000，厨工算3个，工资4500*3=13500，水电煤按6000一个月，不算损耗，则一个月利润是18.9万*60%-9000-13500-6000=<strong>84900元</strong>，这个数字让我对线下店重又燃起希望！</p><p>客流就那么多，这间做起来，周边就会有几间贴上旺铺招租，商业竞争真是残酷，对门烧腊店面积大概比猪脚饭店多个1/3，未来烧腊店倒了搬过去，营业额又能更上一层楼，过几个月再去观察。</p><p>铁打的房东流水的店铺，过去一年这条巷经历了无数次洗牌，如果没有猪脚饭老板的屠龙技，有钱的话还是当房东保险一点<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（手动狗头）</span>。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg"
