class="text-big-title">1</h3><p>纸片人美少女把手伸到脸前，比出可爱的手势，像是面对镜头有些羞涩……</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184703549320.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184703549320.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>喜爱动漫的人们，总是能被类似的构图击中过心房。因为这样的亲密视角，会让人感到自己与屏幕中“老婆”的距离被无限拉近。</p><p>可一旦你知道真相，反而会觉得离“老婆”们愈发遥远。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184705556850.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="506" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184705556850.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">巨 手 怪<br></p><p><strong>这是3D动画行业里，不少人避而不谈的内幕——</strong></p><p>你看到的，可能只有表面的美好：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184705339915.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="365" data-h="284" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184705339915.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>背地里暗藏的“残酷“真相，或许会让人二次元梦碎：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184706978570.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="354" data-h="265" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184706978570.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而这些也只不过是3D动画内幕中的九牛一毛。</p><p>作为观众的我们，常常因荧幕上一些极具表现力的动画镜头而感到震撼，但却很难想到，它们背后究竟有多少让人意想不到的“欺骗性”技巧……</p><p>例如，你的亲亲纸片人老婆，很可能有着一个歪嘴建模。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184707662166.gif" data-w="449" data-h="278" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184707662166.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>‍</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">2</h3><p>近些日子，日本一位动画爱好者さんご公布了其自制动画《Bite the Bullet》。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184718308656.gif" data-w="486" data-h="238" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184718308656.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>该自制动画一经发出，很快就成为了日本动漫圈的热门话题。人们首先赞叹的，是它展现出来的高质量。</p><p>但这还不是结束。因为作者さんご也随之分享了《Bite the Bullet》的一部分制作过程。</p><p>而这些制作过程图所展现出来的内容，则让大部分观众感到始料未及——</p><p>例如，几帧“侧面角色在说话”的动画背后，其实藏着的是一个歪嘴建模。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184722814138.gif" data-w="558" data-h="212" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184722814138.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>唯美的少女俯视镜头，是靠着畸形人的演绎。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184724676540.gif" data-w="563" data-h="241" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184724676540.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>3D动画的神秘面纱，随着这些制作图的出现而被揭下。在了解到这些“残酷”的真相后，大量网友都发出了“原来如此”的感叹。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184725535213.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="600" data-h="341" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184725535213.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>但对于3D动画来说，这只不过是最初极的“诈骗”技法。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184728452635.gif" data-w="499" data-h="246" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184728452635.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>正如我们所见，大部分动漫都有一种不细想不会觉得奇怪的景象：</p><p>角色明明露出的是侧脸，但却能看到一张完整的嘴。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184731872074.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="389" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184731872074.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这是一种降低作画成本的表现方式。随着大量的沿用，如今已经成为让观众们见怪不怪的现象。</p><p>而一部分3D动画<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（尤其是三渲二）</span>为了达成同样的效果，就不得不在调整模型的过程中特意把嘴往歪了放。</p><p><strong>这是较为反直觉的一种作画方式，但却是3D动画的常见技巧之一：为了所呈现画面的表现力，而“破坏”建模本身的完整。</strong></p><p>简而言之——在观众看不到的地方，建模人可能没你想象的那么“协调”。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184731221936.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="480" data-h="173" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184731221936.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>随着越来越多3D动画的制作方公布出自家作品的创作过程，这些藏在背后的秘密也开始逐渐被揭晓。</p><p>例如在最终成品中颇有气势的怒吼画面。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184731750411.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="500" data-h="282" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184731750411.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>正面看起来可能会有些滑稽。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184732778844.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="500" data-h="283" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184732778844.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>三渲二动画《舞武器舞乱伎》的CG总监铃木大介就曾就该作品中的一幕对此进行过详细解释：</p><p>“……这张图使用插件给轮廓线增加了笔触，而模型里并不包含的后槽牙则是重新捏了之后放进去的。这就是一个典型的例子——<strong>为了看起来像2D，而将3D模型进行了精细化处理。”</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184732203206.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="640" data-h="326" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184732203206.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>观众是只能看到最终呈现画面的。而为了将特定角度的效果演绎到极致，作画师常常需要不惜代价地打破模型的一致性。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184733647055.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="576" data-h="662" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184733647055.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>对于大部分3D作品而言，在观众看不到的地方，实际情况可能与你想象的大有不同。</p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">3</h3><p>一位玩家对于游戏《龙珠斗士Z》的挖掘，就曾经把一些普通观众看不到的内容展现了出来。</p><p>《龙珠战士Z》是由万代南宫梦发行的一款动作格斗类游戏。作为一款漫改作品，该作极为重视游戏中的格斗演出，也因此大获好评。</p><p>而游戏虽然为传统的2D横版格斗游戏视角，但人物建模均为3D，且会根据技能的不同有短暂的视角转换及演出。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184738498048.gif" data-w="569" data-h="381" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184738498048.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>一位名为sigmaG19的YouTube up主，就通过了一些技巧将游戏画面视角进行了固定，随后分享出了一些些更为“真实”的游戏画面。</p><p>在对比之下，人们才明白有些张力极强的演出是如何实现的。</p><p>最经典的例子，就是孙悟饭的大招动画。在实机过程中，它看起来是这样的：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184741532555.gif" data-w="935" data-h="550" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184741532555.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这段动画特意给了角色手部一个特写镜头，让整体动作看起来极具张力。而如果在固定视角下观看这段演出，你会发现上图的画面其实是通过让双手猛然增大的方式来实现的：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184745460697.gif" data-w="615" data-h="356" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184745460697.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>类似的动画片段还有不少。一旦少了镜头和视角的加持，实际画面效果就会略显奇怪。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184756724170.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="480" data-h="587" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184756724170.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>一部分动画行业的从业者，将这种手法戏称为“必要的欺诈”。</strong></p><p>所谓“欺诈”，即是指通过扭曲、增大缩小等手段“破坏”模型本身的一致性，从而达到加强画面张力的目的。</p><p>女性向游戏《IDOLiSH7》CG动画的制作者曾展示过类似的手段。</p><p>在该游戏的一段动画CG中，有一个角色侧面略露翘臀的画面：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184757906866.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="563" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184757906866.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而为了达成上图中的“丰满”效果，制作组实际上是给原本平平无奇的模型加了臀垫：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184758443897.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="644" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184758443897.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>由于二次元动画经常有类似的夸张效果，所以即使一些动作或效果在实际上并不“合理”，观众也并不会感到特别奇怪。</p><p>所谓不“合理”，即“这些画面其实在现实中无法达成”。</p><p>其中最经典的代表画面，就是奥特曼的标准动作：</p><p>奥特曼举着拳头向前飞行。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184758065538.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="320" data-h="240" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184758065538.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>奥特曼呼之欲出的大拳头，给整个画面带来了极具动态的冲击感。但如果要在现实里实现这个姿势，就必须接受让奥特曼比例失调。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184759666909.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="480" data-h="540" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184759666909.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在2D视角里，英雄奥特曼一往无前。但在3D世界中，它其实更像是路飞。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184800831264.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1256" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184800831264.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>这种为了画面更有表现力而不合理地强调部分肢体的技法，如今已经有了一个专有名词：谎言透视<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（嘘パース）</span>。</strong></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184801618016.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="366" data-h="367" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184801618016.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184802499362.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="363" data-h="346" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184802499362.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>对于动漫爱好者来说，你大概率曾被谎言透视“骗”过无数次，但其实并未察觉。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184802991359.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="960" data-h="769" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184802991359.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><h3 label="大标题" class="text-big-title">4</h3><p>动漫爱好者恐怕都对一种画面印象深刻：角色举着拳头或武器伸到屏幕前：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184804864189.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="400" data-h="462" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184804864189.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>你可能会隐隐觉得这个构图可能不太合理，但并未想过究竟有多不合理。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184804171945.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="500" data-h="470" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184804171945.png?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/png/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在一部分强调画面冲击感的2D动画中，这种画面就经常被人吐槽“很难想到是怎么做出这种姿势的”。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184805069720.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="800" data-h="600" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184805069720.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184805757670.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1000" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184805757670.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而3D动画的内幕给出了解答：硬凹。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184806669823.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="1050" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184806669823.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>动漫《高分少女》中就有这样一幕：女主角给男主角脸上狠狠来了一拳。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184807556697.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="849" data-h="509" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184807556697.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>观众们在作品中只能看到这样的短暂一幕，便会自动脑补画面外的部分，并自然而然地将其合理化。</p><p>但如果你认真考究就能发现，这种画面在现实里实现不了。而3D动画为了让它成真，会用硬凹造型的方式来达成。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184807980627.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="664" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184807980627.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p label="图片备注" class="text-img-note">（如果只使用广角镜头来达成类似效果的话，人物面部也会跟着扭曲）<br></p><p>我们在3D作品中常看到的类似镜头，基本上都是通过扭曲、修改建模肢体来实现的。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184809056120.gif" data-w="534" data-h="294" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184809056120.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>这种无伤大雅的“欺骗”手段，能让原本平平无奇的画面变得更具2D动画的夸张感。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184810192682.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="480" data-h="828" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184810192682.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>在很多意想不到的地方，你的眼睛可能已经被虚晃过一枪。</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184810392901.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="703" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184810392901.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184811456903.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="1000" data-h="703" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184811456903.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>而这种谎言透视，也的确能极大提高画面的观赏性。一位建模师曾分享出调整角色脚部大小前后的对比图，效果高下立判：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184814309158.gif" data-w="641" data-h="510" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184814309158.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184816158065.gif" data-w="641" data-h="510" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184816158065.gif" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p>也是因为这种极佳的效果，谎言透视如今已经无处不在。</p><p>另外值得一提的是，<strong>这种“不合理”的夸张技巧，其实早在数十年前就开始被人使用。</strong></p><p>在1970年代到1980年代中期，一位名为金田伊功的画师对日本动画界产生了巨大影响。他参与制作了《盖塔机器人G》《聪明的一休》《鬼太郎》等多部经典动画，并以其独特的作画技巧而闻名。</p><p>在金田伊功的作画过程中，他常使用一种特殊的技巧：夸大人物/物品的远近关系——将近的物品极致放大，又将远处的物品极致缩小。</p><p>经过这样处理的画面，显然有很多透视错误。但它的画面表现力却让人折服：</p><p class="img-center-box"><img class="lazyImg" _src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184817840169.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" data-w="400" data-h="291" src="https://img.huxiucdn.com/article/content/202203/23/184817840169.jpg?imageView2/2/w/1000/format/jpg/interlace/1/q/85" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></p><p><strong>这种作画方式，给整个业界的造成影响恐怕已经大到难以估量。它也被称为“金田透视法”<span class="text-remarks" label="备注">（金田パース）</span>，属于谎言透视法的奠基石。</strong></p><p>直至今日，这些数不清的“欺瞒”方式，仍然充斥在各个作品之中。</p><p>虽然观众们知道真相后，恐怕都会大呼“诈骗”，但人们心中倒不会有丝毫责怪的意思。</p><p>无论如何，这些都是属于二次元的，善意的谎言。</p><p><span class="text-remarks"
