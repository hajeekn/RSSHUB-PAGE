<strong>猫咪便秘的原因是什么？</strong><br>从从猫-在线咨询的回答<br><p data-pid="jbMgcVwm">猫咪便秘的原因猫咪跟人一样,由于饮食及日常生活环境的原因,也会出现便秘的情况。</p><p data-pid="LFs7RVNJ">猫咪便秘是猫咪消化系统疾病中最为常见的病症之一,<b>是干硬的粪便在直肠中堆积难以排出导致的</b>。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-7103545cbe906a71f09ae887b8df5208_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="1036" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-a4396dbbf25abd5a6ba93c287329f2f9_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-7103545cbe906a71f09ae887b8df5208_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="yrij73Bv">猫咪便秘的原因有:<b>肥胖、消瘦、水源问题、毛球症、异食癖、卫生差、菊花受伤、应激反应、年龄大、天生骨骼异常&直肠狭窄、内分泌失常、药物、猫粮、心理问题等等。</b></p><p data-pid="AjnDhY31"><b>解决办法</b></p><p data-pid="2CJwvMjq">1.多喝水，没事让猫咪多喝水</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-c90de9ea99625311e8dfa072f230b9ca_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="649" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-4e9362218ca93d98e9b16a28b77f2796_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-c90de9ea99625311e8dfa072f230b9ca_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="GGyjhqH7">2.多运动，至少每天10分钟</p><p data-pid="5ikU4RQZ">3.科学饮食，不能喂不好的猫粮</p><p data-pid="ZqpkxTHj">4.对于毛球这种情况，可以给猫咪喂食猫草和化毛膏</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-8b8dde4ebee1351173d77498bba59f75_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="1016" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-0cd2d286d8833ee2beab2d4f98425790_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-8b8dde4ebee1351173d77498bba59f75_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p></p>
