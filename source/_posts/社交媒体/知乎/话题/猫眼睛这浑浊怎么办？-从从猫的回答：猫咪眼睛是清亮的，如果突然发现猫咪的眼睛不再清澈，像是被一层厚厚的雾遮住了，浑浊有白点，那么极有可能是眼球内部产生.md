<strong>猫眼睛这浑浊怎么办？</strong><br>从从猫的回答<br><p data-pid="0mD3AvvB">猫咪眼睛是清亮的，如果突然发现猫咪的眼睛不再清澈，像是被一层厚厚的雾遮住了，浑浊有白点，那么极有可能是眼球内部产生了病变，针对这种情况一定要及时检查处理，不然猫咪很有可能会失明。</p><p data-pid="OERa6Axs">以角膜浑浊、角膜赘生、角膜溃疡、角膜穿孔及留有角膜斑为特征,长毛猫更容易患此病。</p><p data-pid="wa9E0wSp">猫咪眼睛浑浊是什么原因？</p><p data-pid="JMdGb5ri">瞳孔如果发生颜色变化：可以考虑白内障、玻璃体出血、视网膜剥离等情况。</p><p data-pid="jWcpjv8W">赶紧送去看看医生。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-ceef544046331546020d539d913683db_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="568" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-59a5ec38dd68eee7d8c9d697088a3574_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-ceef544046331546020d539d913683db_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-a4a7f982d8a1bbe9bad036f673d0c4f6_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="1016" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-0cd2d286d8833ee2beab2d4f98425790_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-a4a7f982d8a1bbe9bad036f673d0c4f6_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-bf53eebc09ec008d7f6e500bc8294403_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="853" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-37623f9344e608b3b5e78f04abb7e0e7_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-bf53eebc09ec008d7f6e500bc8294403_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p></p>
