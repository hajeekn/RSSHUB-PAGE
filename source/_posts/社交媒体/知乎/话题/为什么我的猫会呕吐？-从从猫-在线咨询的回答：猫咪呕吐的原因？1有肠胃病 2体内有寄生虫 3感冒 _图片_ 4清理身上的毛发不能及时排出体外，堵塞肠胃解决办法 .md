<strong>为什么我的猫会呕吐？</strong><br>从从猫-在线咨询的回答<br><p data-pid="8ip0taTT"><b>猫咪呕吐的原因？</b></p><p data-pid="QS7CHHoB">1.有肠胃病</p><p data-pid="Bto7oNIO">2.体内有寄生虫</p><p data-pid="FJlENSxM">3感冒</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-c90de9ea99625311e8dfa072f230b9ca_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="649" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-4e9362218ca93d98e9b16a28b77f2796_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-c90de9ea99625311e8dfa072f230b9ca_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="LG-aXicY"><b>4.清理身上的毛发不能及时排出体外，堵塞肠胃</b></p><p data-pid="FqTI6De2">解决办法</p><p data-pid="28eZTm4F"><b>1.若是猫咪得了肠胃病，检查猫粮是否与猫咪肠胃病有关，如果是，换用对猫咪肠胃无害的猫粮；不随便给猫咪喂食。</b>如果不算严重可以先少量喂猫咪一些可食用的消炎药或助消化药，若是严重最好先将猫咪送往兽医处进行检查，然后再进行针对性治疗。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-77345791a76f6448518cca13f74b6aa6_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="1280" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-fa8e36e4d5b263dc3f2f5386ae95ebfa_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-77345791a76f6448518cca13f74b6aa6_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="nLW3mGni">2.定期驱虫</p><p data-pid="iCG02B5S">3.多注意给猫咪保暖。</p><p data-pid="l2WXiOnI"><b>4.可以让猫咪吃一些青草、植物油、化毛膏后，将肚内毛团吐出来。另外平时可以多为猫咪梳毛，避免猫咪食入过多杂毛。</b></p><p data-pid="JP4pPxQe">5.如果得了猫瘟一定要去医院</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-de55d1210ef2bd66fd5d50ea1fa27829_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="853" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-5980a1d2630e8e076ae4cd03c4fc43bb_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-de55d1210ef2bd66fd5d50ea1fa27829_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p></p>
