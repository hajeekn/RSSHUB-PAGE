<strong>猫会感冒吗？</strong><br>从从猫-在线咨询的回答<br><p data-pid="Dsd475il"><b>猫感冒是猫受到寒冷刺激引起的以发热和上呼吸道黏膜卡他性炎性疾病。</b></p><p data-pid="uNKo6q-5"><b>一.病症：</b></p><p data-pid="3GsLRNhd"><b>1.体温升高、</b>喷嚏</p><p data-pid="F33SlRfk">2.鼻塞、流鼻涕、咳嗽、发抖</p><p data-pid="2mifgrAz">3.呼吸不顺畅、呼吸声音较大</p><p data-pid="C7WakExK">4.精神委靡、食欲下降等。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-337677f6ac3268be965085fb30c492c8_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="853" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-0c030a78114625d9313c3da60c42a4a4_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-337677f6ac3268be965085fb30c492c8_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="09udQcK3"><b>二.病因：</b></p><p data-pid="B7TjqKz8">1.天气变化，一时冷一时热，主人没有留意，猫咪就感冒了</p><p data-pid="2u83g3lj">2.主人给猫咪洗澡之后，没有把它的猫毛吹干</p><p data-pid="iMU1eXmM">3.不适应外面的温度</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-8b8dde4ebee1351173d77498bba59f75_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="1016" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-0cd2d286d8833ee2beab2d4f98425790_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-8b8dde4ebee1351173d77498bba59f75_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="ADFK9r4X"><b>三.解决办法</b></p><p data-pid="88rM6nER">1.多多晒晒太阳，提高抵抗力</p><p data-pid="KNdJuXpG">2.给猫咪的窝添加被子</p><p data-pid="F-dmNHzG">3.多喝水，多运动</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3127e94d9d0bf3e415035ccb945bc460_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1280" data-rawheight="853" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-8fc009b3cc32c1a3c94b3be52d06b38f_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-3127e94d9d0bf3e415035ccb945bc460_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p></p>
