手术预约时间要避免在发情期内</p><p data-pid="riJoqqOC">✅ 狗狗已做过完整的免疫</p><p data-pid="DKGsU9p0">✅ 状态良好，没有呕吐、拉稀、发热、咳嗽、食欲不振、精神沉郁等现象</p><p data-pid="x-6Nqddj">✅ 身体健康，没有肝病、肾病、糖尿病、心脏病等慢性疾病</p><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-1f6ffe0bc698d5fc24f49dfd195fef1f_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="224" data-rawheight="299" class="content_image" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><h3>提前准备术后贴心小物</h3><p data-pid="YRdOiu2s">✅ 伊丽莎白圈：防止狗狗舔伤口，口水造成伤口发炎</p><p data-pid="DjAz6Gi6">✅ data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-5a812dc1520ea63378c72b6857421909_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="5472" data-rawheight="3648" class="origin_image
