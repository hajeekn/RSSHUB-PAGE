<strong>为什么那么多人允许动物上床？</strong><br>我吃香菜的回答<br><p data-pid="cScTVW0k">以前养的猫，后来分手，女朋友拿养去了。</p><p data-pid="d3PU23hq">给她准备的猫窝，从来不睡。</p><p data-pid="ZoVxFDJT">我一睡觉，她不是爬我肚子上，就是窝在我头顶，要不就床头蹦迪跑酷。</p><p data-pid="3Q0KAfo0">也不想让她上来，实在是没办法，后来买个了立体猫笼，睡觉前关他们进去。</p><p data-pid="-fyCBJUW">然后他们晚上就猫笼里面跑酷，铁笼子噼里啪啦一直响，睡眠堪忧。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-1b89383c4f7ce6506ea920574ccac96a_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="960" data-rawheight="1280" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-f2630f52bc3fb25d56fb4ad08ad315cb_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-1b89383c4f7ce6506ea920574ccac96a_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-dbce2ed1bbe7bd48844fca52736f6227_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="960" data-rawheight="1280" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-ebdbeaf06076e36cc9fabf0b007861f5_b.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-dbce2ed1bbe7bd48844fca52736f6227_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p></p>
