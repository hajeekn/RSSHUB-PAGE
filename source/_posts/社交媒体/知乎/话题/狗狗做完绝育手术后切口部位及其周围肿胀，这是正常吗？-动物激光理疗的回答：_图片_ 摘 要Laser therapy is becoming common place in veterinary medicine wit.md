<strong>狗狗做完绝育手术后切口部位及其周围肿胀，这是正常吗？</strong><br>动物激光理疗的回答<br><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-020c25369585c7fdbcefa80601add377_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="707" data-rawheight="247" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-020c25369585c7fdbcefa80601add377_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><h2><b>摘  要</b></h2><p data-pid="MQClRQ8N">Laser therapy is becoming common place in veterinary medicine with little evidence proving efficacy or dosages. This study evaluated surgical wound healing in canines.</p><p data-pid="3igPvjU2">激光疗法在兽医学中越来越普遍，但几乎没有相关试验证据证明其功效或规范治疗剂量。这项研究评估了使用激光治疗犬的手术伤口愈合情况。</p><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><p data-pid="QQlMVAx3">Twelve Dachshunds underwent thoraco-lumbar hemilaminectomies for intervertebral disc disease (IVDD). Digital photographs were taken of their incisions within 24 h of surgery and 1, 3, 5, 7, and 21 days postoperatively. The first three dogs were used to create a standardized scar scale to score the other dogs' incision healing. The remaining 9 dogs were randomly assigned to either receive 8 J/cm2 laser therapy once a day for 7 days or the non-laser treated control group. </p><p data-pid="dYjHbJGM">12 只腊肠犬因椎间盘疾病 (IVDD) 接受了胸腰椎半椎板切除术。在手术后 24 小时内和术后 1、3、5、7 和 21 天拍摄他们切口的照片。根据前三只狗的切口愈合情况制作一个标准化的疤痕量表，作为评分标准，以此来评估其他狗的切口愈合情况。其余9只狗被随机分为两组，一组接受8J/c㎡激光治疗，每天一次，持续7天；另一组作为对照组接受非激光治疗。</p><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><p data-pid="ncKRHAHJ">Incision healing was scored based on the scar scale from 0 to 5, with zero being a fresh incision and five being completely healed with scar contraction and hair growth. All scar scores significantly improved with increasing time from surgery (<0.001). Good agreement was achieved for inter-rater reliability (p = 0.9). </p><p data-pid="kBXNxabw">切口愈合评分表根据疤痕收缩状态和毛发生长情况，从0到5分为5个等级，0为新鲜切口，5为完全愈合。随着手术后治疗时间的增加，所有疤痕状态均显著改善（<0.001）。评估者间可靠性取得了良好的一致性（p=0.9）。</p><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><p data-pid="P6J_aaJg">Laser therapy increased the scar scale score, showed improved cosmetic healing, by day seven and continued to be significantly increased on day 21 compared to control dogs (p < 0.001). </p><p data-pid="ND5UY6-m">激光治疗提高了疤痕量表的评分数值，与对照犬相比，到第七天展现出更优的切口愈合外观。一直到第21天，疤痕量表评分数值继续显着增加（p<0.001）。</p><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><p data-pid="ccBK7ZHh">Daily application of laser therapy at 8J/cm2 hastened wound healing in Dachshunds that received thoracolumbar hemilaminectomies for IVDD. It also improved the cosmetic appearance.</p><p data-pid="PE2ttsm3">由实验结果推导出结论：每天使用8J/c㎡的激光治疗加速了因IVDD接受胸腰椎半椎板切除术的腊肠犬的伤口愈合，它还减少了疤痕增生。</p><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-1ce4d72045192fe5ae984de36694ce43_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="406" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-1ce4d72045192fe5ae984de36694ce43_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="73i2cTpD">Figure 1 </p><p data-pid="sHErVo6e">Examples of the scar scale images used for reviewer ranking of surgical incisions for both treatment groups. (B) This image was defined as a scar scale score of five.</p><p data-pid="FsfaFSro">图1。用于评估两组手术切口愈合状况等级评分表的例子。(A)在等级评分表中该图像被定义为分0。(B)在等级评分表中此图像定义为5分。</p><p class="ztext-empty-paragraph"><br></p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-ada1a273e23b21f9176b35aae2f1764c_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="272" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-ada1a273e23b21f9176b35aae2f1764c_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="H5Y_nFgl">Figure 2 </p><p data-pid="RwEpC-dp">Representative images of the two treatments groups at day 21.
