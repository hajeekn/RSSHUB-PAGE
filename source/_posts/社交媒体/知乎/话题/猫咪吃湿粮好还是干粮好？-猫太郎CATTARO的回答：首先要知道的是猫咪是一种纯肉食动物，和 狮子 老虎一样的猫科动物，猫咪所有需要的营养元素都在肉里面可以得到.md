<strong>猫咪吃湿粮好还是干粮好？</strong><br>猫太郎CATTARO的回答<br><p data-pid="vOtCG8N4">首先要知道的是猫咪是一种纯肉食动物，和<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E7%8B%AE%E5%AD%90&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2219995381%7D" class="internal">狮子</a>老虎一样的猫科动物，猫咪所有需要的营养元素都在肉里面可以得到，所以一款好的猫粮是由动物蛋白和其他维生素和营养元素构成，<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E5%8A%A8%E7%89%A9%E8%9B%8B%E7%99%BD&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2219995381%7D" class="internal">动物蛋白</a>越高，对猫咪来说越棒(๑•̀ㅂ•́) ✧。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-1e8072d8fef5f2b0bb346e9d90d9e5e4_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal" data-rawwidth="1080" data-rawheight="1440" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-1e8072d8fef5f2b0bb346e9d90d9e5e4_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="2T9ykGSy">其实在猫咪的饮食习惯上，<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E6%B9%BF%E7%B2%AE&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2219995381%7D" class="internal">湿粮</a>比干粮会更胜一筹。湿粮通常是指主食罐或者湿猫粮，水分含量高，通常达到70%以上，因为天性的原因很多猫咪有不爱喝水的毛病，在野外猫科动物大部分所需水分都是来自捕猎的食物，这部分水分一般占所需水分的60-70%，主食罐很好的迎合了猫咪的天性，也可以增加猫咪日常水分的摄取。原料取材是新鲜的肉类，动物营养蛋白含量高，不加入<a href="https://www.zhihu.com/search?q=%E7%A2%B3%E6%B0%B4%E5%8C%96%E5%90%88%E7%89%A9&search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_source=Entity&hybrid_search_extra=%7B%22sourceType%22%3A%22answer%22%2C%22sourceId%22%3A2219995381%7D" class="internal">碳水化合物</a>，这其实更适合猫咪的饮食习惯。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic2.zhimg.com/v2-6211a64c84a09469fd3b6d51cac310c5_1440w.jpg" data-caption data-size="normal"
