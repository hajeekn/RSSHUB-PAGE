英子001的回答<br><br><p data-pid="pVBQ7Pcg">在扬州，国货很多，而且有名。只要走进东关街，里面简直是扬州国货的集中地。</p><p data-pid="ZQmSt9Ak">扬州三把刀、扬州谢馥春、扬州炒饭、扬州狮子头、扬州包子、扬州老鹅、扬州牛皮糖等等。</p><p data-pid="yCuq-94E">在这些国货中，我最想安利给全世界的当属扬州牛皮糖了。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-05d6a6ac5f3fff94117b2839819aa117_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="850" data-rawheight="681" data-size="normal" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-6b72f271447bacd8a57853d708506d9d_720w.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-05d6a6ac5f3fff94117b2839819aa117_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="gVZNhaZ0">当然不是因为别的不好，而是这些国货中，牛皮糖相对好运输。</p><p data-pid="bq23KJqj">每年要回老家时，老家亲人就会给我说，你就带几盒扬州牛皮糖回来吧。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-fd31379ea05afdc3d42f4923db9ca027_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="1024" data-rawheight="321" data-size="normal" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic1.zhimg.com/v2-0433f3887a91fcf804120db0677c95da_720w.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-fd31379ea05afdc3d42f4923db9ca027_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="emHsRLRQ">看来，扬州牛皮糖在我的那些亲戚眼中，已是扬州美食的代表了。</p><p data-pid="_S8caWKw">牛皮糖号称"扬州一绝"，是扬州特产。</p><p data-pid="aV72hg7e">它甜而不腻，外层裹着一层芝麻，切面呈半透明状，弹性十足，味道香甜，细嚼时不粘牙。</p><figure data-size="normal"><img src="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-684f24fd90bd55d1f9a4a1a522f05b14_1440w.jpg" data-rawwidth="550" data-rawheight="299" data-size="normal" data-default-watermark-src="https://pic3.zhimg.com/v2-8a10ab74ac2f0b108e6289cfcc5e00d3_720w.jpg" class="origin_image zh-lightbox-thumb" data-original="https://pic4.zhimg.com/v2-684f24fd90bd55d1f9a4a1a522f05b14_r.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></figure><p data-pid="fZPYkGl5">正是它香甜的口味，让人嚼之一口，回味无穷，而成为了扬州特产。</p>
